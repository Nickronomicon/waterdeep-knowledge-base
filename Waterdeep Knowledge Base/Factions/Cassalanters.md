# Cassalanters

The Cassalanters are an old Waterdeep noble banking family. They entered table truth directly in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]], when [[People/NPCs/Victoro Cassalanter]] and [[People/NPCs/Ammalia Cassalanter]] approached the party at the opera.

## Current Status

- Victoro and Ammalia attended the opera at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]].
- Victoro recognized or at least noticed Renaer's rescuers and the new owners of [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Victoro offered the party a conversation about a loan for restoring Trollskull Manor.
- The party was invited to [[Places/Cassalanter Villa]] on Diamond Street in the Sea Ward to discuss terms.

## Table-Facing Impression

- Victoro was polished, confident, and socially selective, shaking hands with the human men while giving Broethuul an odd look and overlooking Davy.
- Ammalia projected strong noble presence and social gravity.
- An older tiefling woman in black attended Ammalia but was not introduced.

## DM-Facing Relevance

The Cassalanters are already tied to the campaign's private Crommor material through [[Threads/Cassalanter Warning in the Bugle]], but the party-facing opera scene only establishes them as nobles, bankers, and possible lenders.
