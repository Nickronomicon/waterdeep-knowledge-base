# Xanathar Guild

The Xanathar Guild is a violent underworld faction currently fighting the [[Factions/Zhentarim]] in Waterdeep.

## Current Campaign State

- Eye/starburst tattoos mark Xanathar-linked bodies or thugs.
- [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] and his companions in session 1 used eye-tattoo imagery.
- Xanathar-linked bodies appeared in the Dock Ward street violence and [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]].
- A kenku in the warehouse repeated "Xanathar sends his regards."
- Took [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] from the Zhentarim warehouse and moved him to [[Places/Xanathar Sewer Hideout]].
- Uses yellow circle-and-spoke markings as sewer-route signs.
- Session 3 confirmed goblin sentries, a gazer lookout, [[People/NPCs/Grum'shar]], [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]], and an intellect devourer in one sewer hideout.
- Vajra says Xanathar likes to release monsters to spread panic and pull the Watch thin.
- Session 5's Dock Ward grell incident fits that pattern and produced a receipt marked with one staring eye inside a ring.
- In session 6, [[People/NPCs/Vincent Trench]] said that a gazer sent to look at the party "always leads back to Xanathar," though this is Vincent's professional judgment rather than direct proof.
- The [[Objects/Scrap of Note]] includes "X" in a context the party speculated might mean Xanathar, but that is not confirmed.
- In session 8, [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]] reported that [[People/NPCs/Davil Starsong]] and [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]] discussed hiring spies to locate Xanathar Guild hideouts.

## Known Clues

- "Follow the yellow signs in the sewers."
- Xanathar operatives may operate above ground at night and below ground during the day.
- A portal pedestal marked with an eye-like symbol can be activated with a removable sphere, at least by Nihiloor.
- [[Objects/Dockside Transfer Receipt]] for three ironbound crates.
- [[Objects/Loose It Then Leave Note Fragment]] found by the cracked-open [[Objects/Ironbound Grell Crate]].
- A gazer-like creature watched [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] and the alley while the party was at [[Places/Blackstaff Tower]].

## Open Questions

- Where did Nihiloor's portal lead?
- What does the guild know about Renaer, Dagult, and the missing dragons?
- What are Krentz and the deep gnome trying to barricade below the hideout?
- Where are the other ironbound crates, and were they all monster-release assets?
- Who redacted the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]] ownership chain?
- Which Xanathar operative, if any, sent the gazer to watch Trollskull Manor?
- Does "X" in the Scrap of Note mean Xanathar?
- Which hideouts are Davil and Yagra trying to find, and will that spy network expose places the party has already touched?
