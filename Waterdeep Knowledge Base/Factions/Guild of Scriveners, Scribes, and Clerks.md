# Guild of Scriveners, Scribes, and Clerks

The Guild of Scriveners, Scribes, and Clerks maintains or provides access to property records and other written civic business in Waterdeep. Elior and Jonathan visited the guild in [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]] to identify the owner of the suspicious Dock Ward warehouse.

## Known Facts

- The party treated the guild as the proper place to pull property ownership records.
- A clerk named [[People/NPCs/Andrew]] found surrounding records but not the ownership chain for the target warehouse.
- A normal record pull cost two silver per property.
- The warehouse record had been redacted, implying influence beyond an ordinary clerk's authority.
