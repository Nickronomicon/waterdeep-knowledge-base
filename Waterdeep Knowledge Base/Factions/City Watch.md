# City Watch

The City Watch is Waterdeep's civic law-enforcement presence.

## Session 2

- Cordon set around a Dock Ward side-street skirmish with about half a dozen bodies.
- Had disarmed and arrested three bloody humans.
- One officer was tempted by Elior's 1 gp bribe, but another knocked the coin away and shut the moment down.
- The party ended the session hearing hobnailed boots approaching [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]], possibly the Watch.

## Session 3

- [[People/NPCs/Hustus Staget]] led the Watch response to [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]].
- Staget took statements, accepted the party as rescuers rather than suspects, and gave them copies of the [[Reference/Waterdeep Code Legal]].
- He said the Watch could not go into the sewers while gang warfare was active above ground.
- His practical advice: keep the blood off the streets.
