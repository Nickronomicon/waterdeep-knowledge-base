# Bregan D'aerthe

Bregan D'aerthe is Jarlaxle Baenre's drow mercenary/intelligence network. At the table, the faction is currently visible through [[People/NPCs/Zardoz Zord]], [[Places/Sea Maidens Fair]], and the broader harbor presence, but its full identity and role in the Grand Game remain unrevealed to the party.

## Current Table Status

- Zardoz Zord was introduced socially at the opera.
- The Sea Maidens ships are in Waterdeep Harbor.
- [[People/NPCs/Zelifarn]] and the session 12 harbor activity make the ships and underwater infrastructure easier to bring back into focus.
- The party does not yet know the Bregan D'aerthe identity, Jarlaxle identity, nimblewright surveillance structure, or Luskan political agenda.

## Remix Role

- Jarlaxle begins outside the Stone/Eye race, then can discover it through nimblewright records, party questions, or faction intelligence.
- Bregan D'aerthe can become a rival, ally, information broker, or heist target.
- Their main structural sites are Fenerus Stormcastle's House, Seven Masks Theater, and the Sea Maidens Faire / ships.
- Their response teams are drow-led and clue-rich; see [[Reference/Remix Response Teams]].

## Current Prep Use

- If the party follows Zardoz, asks about ships, investigates nimblewrights, or returns to Zelifarn, use [[Reference/Remix Eye Heists]] and [[Reference/Remix Faction Outposts]].
- If the Stone / Great Game clock advances through harbor activity, Bregan D'aerthe can notice the party before the party notices them.
