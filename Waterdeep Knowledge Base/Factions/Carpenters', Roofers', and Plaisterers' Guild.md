# Carpenters', Roofers', and Plaisterers' Guild

The Carpenters', Roofers', and Plaisterers' Guild is the guild handling structural repair estimates and legal serviceability concerns for [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Session 4

- Elior visited the guild hall to request an inspection.
- [[People/NPCs/Sukov]] arrived later that day with two apprentices.
- The guild estimated 250 dragons to make Trollskull Manor structurally fit for trade.
- Sukov emphasized that these were guild rates and that the guild does not bargain much.
- The guild has the practical power to block or condemn unsafe buildings.
- Sukov hinted that [[Places/Frewn's Brews]] has questionable non-guild roof work.

## Session 10

- The 250 gp roof/walls/plaster estimate was restated during the Cassalanter loan discussion.
- The roof was expected to take at least a week, and other work such as windows should not begin until roof and walls are repaired.
- Victoro Cassalanter offered to have relevant guild representatives present when the loan money and documents arrive.
