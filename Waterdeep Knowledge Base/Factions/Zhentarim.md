# Zhentarim

The Zhentarim, also called the Black Network or "the Zhents" at the table, are an organized criminal faction active in Waterdeep's underworld.

## Current Campaign State

- Openly fighting the [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] in the streets as of [[Sessions/Session 02 - 2026-02-22]].
- Marked by black flying-snake tattoos.
- Abducted [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] and [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] outside [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]].
- Used [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] as a safe house or holding location.
- Questioned Renaer about Dagult Neverember and the rumored cache of embezzled dragons.
- Were then hit by a Xanathar-linked raid that took Floon.

## Open Questions

- Which local Zhentarim cell staged the abduction?
- Who ordered the search for Renaer and the locket contents?
- How does this cell relate to Davil Starsong and other possible Zhentarim factions?
