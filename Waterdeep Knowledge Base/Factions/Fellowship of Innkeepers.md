# Fellowship of Innkeepers

The Fellowship of Innkeepers is the Waterdeep guild relevant to operating [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] as a tavern or inn. [[People/NPCs/Broxley Fairkettle]] introduced the guild track directly in [[Sessions/Session 08 - 2026-04-30]].

## Current Campaign State

- Broxley visited Trollskull Manor to discuss possible reopening and guild membership.
- Guild dues were estimated at about 10 gp per tenday.
- The guild provides or enforces rules, hours, guidelines, and operating norms.
- Broxley warned that other innkeepers may dislike a non-guild business opening.
- [[Places/Frewn's Brews]] is a guild member, though apparently late on dues.
- During session 10 cost review, annual membership was stated as 25 gp per year; this conflicts with or supersedes the earlier rough 10 gp per tenday estimate and should be confirmed when the guild representatives arrive.

## Open Questions

- Has Trollskull Manor joined the Fellowship yet?
- What specific bylaws will apply to the party's tavern plans?
- Will Frewn use guild channels against the party?
- What final dues figure is table-canonical once signing/payment happens?
