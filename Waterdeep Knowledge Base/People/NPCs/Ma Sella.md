# Ma Sella

Ma Sella is a Dock Ward fishwife and long-standing local fixture. [[People/NPCs/Vajra Safahr]] named her as the best witness to the monster disturbance investigated in [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]].

## Known Facts

- Sells fish in the Dock Ward and is easy for locals to point out.
- Built like a bollard and blunt in speech.
- Saw the grell drop from a warehouse roof near Fish Street/Fishgut Court.
- Described the creature as tentacled, beaked, floating, and wrong.
- Saw two rough, violent men shouting at it as if it was supposed to obey them.
- Did not see obvious Zhentarim marks on the men, but identified them as muscle rather than dock people, fishermen, or street folk.
- Sold Broethuul a whole fresh mackerel for six silver on Ches 3, 1492 DR.
