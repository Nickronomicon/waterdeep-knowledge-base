# Threestrings

Threestrings, full name likely Matrim "Threestrings" Mereg, is a Yawning Portal bard/contact who helped connect the party before the campaign began. In session 4, he also acted as the intermediary for the party's invitation to meet [[People/NPCs/Mirt]].

## Known Role

- The party became acquainted through Bonnie, Threestrings, and Durnan's orbit at [[Places/Yawning Portal|the Yawning Portal]].
- Davy's background names Threestrings as part of his Waterdeep support network.
- Stopped by [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] with brandy in [[Sessions/Session 04 - 2026-03-22]].
- Said [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] spoke well of the party after the Floon rescue.
- Delivered the [[Objects/Opera Invitation from Mirt]] as a paper bird.
- Offered to bring Davy something formal to wear for the opera.
- Sent Bonnie's regards.
