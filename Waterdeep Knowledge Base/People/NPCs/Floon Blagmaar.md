# Floon Blagmaar

Floon Blagmaar is Volo's missing friend and the party's first rescue objective. He is known for wearing [[Objects/Floon's Unicorn Necklace]], a conspicuous unicorn-head medallion on blue pearls.

## Current Status

- Missing as of the end of [[Sessions/Session 02 - 2026-02-22]].
- Likely taken from [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] by [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] operatives.
- [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] believes Floon was caught in a mistaken-identity kidnapping aimed at Renaer.

## Known Facts

- Described by Volo as handsome, early thirties, with wavy red-blond hair.
- Wore a large unicorn medallion on blue pearls.
- Was drinking with Volo and then Renaer at [[Places/Skewered Dragon|the Skewered Dragon]].
- Was extremely drunk when Renaer came to retrieve him.
- Was abducted with Renaer by Zhentarim.
- Was separated from Renaer in the warehouse and later taken by the Xanathar side.
