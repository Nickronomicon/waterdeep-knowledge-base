# Laeral Silverhand

Laeral Silverhand is the Open Lord of Waterdeep. She appeared publicly at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] during [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]].

## Current Status

- Entered the opera shortly before the first act began.
- Arrived with attendants, guards, and [[People/NPCs/Mirt]].
- Her arrival sharpened the room: some attendees moved toward her to seek attention, while others moved to the edges.

## DM-Facing Relevance

Laeral is the intended recipient of [[Objects/The Crommor Warning]], but that connection remains unrevealed to the party.

## Open Questions

- Does Laeral know a warning was meant for her during the Crommor Incident?
- Did she notice the party at the opera beyond their connection to Mirt?
