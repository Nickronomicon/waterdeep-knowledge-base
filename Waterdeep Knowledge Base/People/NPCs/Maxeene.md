# Maxeene

Maxeene is a talking draft mare, Harper contact, and intelligence source at [[Places/Thomril & Sons Cartage]]. [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] sent the party to find her after the opera in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]], and the party interviewed her in [[Sessions/Session 08 - 2026-04-30]].

## Current Status

- Confirmed in transcript truth as a large draft horse who speaks Common when ordinary people are out of earshot.
- Works at Thomril & Sons Cartage and gathers information because people speak freely around animals.
- Survived the session 8 arson attack after being healed by Aladeen and fighting off an attacker herself.
- Knows [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] is the party's home and may stop by if they have hauling work.
- Her information was formalized for the table in [[Objects/Maxeene Information Handout]] and reported to [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] in session 9.
- Remallia said she would see that Maxeene is looked after or employed after the Thomril & Sons disaster.

## Session 8 Information

Zhentarim lead:

- Two days before the party interviewed her, she gave a ride to a sun elf and a half-orc/orc bodyguard, later identified in table discussion as [[People/NPCs/Davil Starsong]] and [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]].
- She dropped them at [[Places/Yawning Portal]].
- They discussed hiring enough spies to root out [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] hideouts in Waterdeep.
- They sounded like rival operators or newcomers making a mark, not Watch officers or public-minded investigators.

Stone lead:

- A rock gnome hired her twice and talked about a significant "stone."
- One ride involved following behind a lord's carriage.
- Another involved following a well-dressed red-haired man on foot while trying to slow the dray down.
- The rides happened at least a week before session 8.
- Maxeene did not know the gnome's name and did not recognize the "Third Eye," "Lady B," or other terms from [[Objects/Scrap of Note]].

## Arson Attack

- Attackers came to Thomril & Sons while the party was interviewing her and appeared to be looking for a spy.
- Aladeen tried to misdirect them with a fake "spy rat" story.
- Maxeene was shot and later stabbed by an attacker carrying poisoned weapons and bearing a flying-snake tattoo.
- Aladeen's Healing Word likely saved her life.
- Maxeene killed the poison-weapon attacker with her hooves.

## Open Questions

- Did the attackers know Maxeene was the spy, or only that a spy worked at Thomril & Sons?
- Where will Maxeene work after Thomril & Sons burned and the stablemaster died?
- Does the rock gnome lead point to Dalakhar, the Stone of Golorr, or another Grand Game actor?
