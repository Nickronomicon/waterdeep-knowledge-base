# Victoro Cassalanter

Victoro Cassalanter is a Waterdeep noble and member of the old banking family [[Factions/Cassalanters]]. He appeared directly at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] during [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]].

## Current Status

- Attended the opera with [[People/NPCs/Ammalia Cassalanter]].
- Approached the party after [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] loudly praised them as his rescuers.
- Recognized or took interest in [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember|Renaer's]] saviors and the new owners of [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Offered to discuss a Cassalanter loan for restoring Trollskull Manor at [[Places/Cassalanter Villa]].
- In session 9, [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] identified Victoro as the only clearly captured name on the party's lender list, reinforcing him as the obvious finance lead.
- In session 10, negotiated [[Objects/Cassalanter Loan|a 2,000 gp loan]] with Aladeen, Broethuul, and Jonathan. The papers are not yet signed.

## Table-Facing Impression

- Polished and socially confident.
- Shook hands with the human men.
- Gave Broethuul an odd look and did not meaningfully acknowledge Davy.
- Spoke favorably about Trollskull Manor's potential.
- In session 10, his voice and manner were smooth, rich, persuasive, and businesslike.
- Suggested opening Trollskull by the festival period around Ches 19-21 to take advantage of Fleet Week/festival traffic.

## DM-Facing Notes

Victoro is central to [[Threads/Cassalanter Warning in the Bugle]] and [[Threads/Crommor Incident]], but those deeper connections are not table-revealed through the Session 7 opera conversation.

## Session 10 Loan Terms

- Principal: 2,000 gp.
- Interest starts the first tenday after Trollskull Manor opens.
- First four months after opening: 2% per tenday.
- Afterward: 3% per tenday.
- All five party members must be present to sign.
- Victoro will send money, documents, and relevant guild representatives to Trollskull Manor the next day.

## Open Questions

- Will all five PCs sign the loan documents?
- Does he recognize Davy, Aladeen, Jonathan, or their Crommor-adjacent significance?
- Did Remallia list him because he is merely practical, because she wants the party near him, or both?
