# Victoro Cassalanter

Victoro Cassalanter is a Waterdeep noble and member of the old banking family [[Factions/Cassalanters]]. He appeared directly at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] during [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]].

## Current Status

- Attended the opera with [[People/NPCs/Ammalia Cassalanter]].
- Approached the party after [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] loudly praised them as his rescuers.
- Recognized or took interest in [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember|Renaer's]] saviors and the new owners of [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Offered to discuss a Cassalanter loan for restoring Trollskull Manor at [[Places/Cassalanter Villa]].

## Table-Facing Impression

- Polished and socially confident.
- Shook hands with the human men.
- Gave Broethuul an odd look and did not meaningfully acknowledge Davy.
- Spoke favorably about Trollskull Manor's potential.

## DM-Facing Notes

Victoro is central to [[Threads/Cassalanter Warning in the Bugle]] and [[Threads/Crommor Incident]], but those deeper connections are not table-revealed through the Session 7 opera conversation.
