# Krentz

Krentz is a bald thug with eye tattoos who confronted [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]] in the Yawning Portal brawl.

## Current Meaning

His eye tattoos became the party's first visual touchstone for [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] markings. In session 2, similar eye/starburst tattoos appeared on bodies in the Dock Ward street violence and the Candle Lane warehouse.
