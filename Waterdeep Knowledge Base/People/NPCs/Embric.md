# Embric

Embric is the fire genasi weaponsmith at [[Places/Steam and Steel]] and [[People/NPCs/Avi|Avi's]] husband.

## Current Role

- Runs Steam and Steel with Avi.
- Handles weaponsmithing.
- Carries standard metal weapons at guild prices.
- Interested in visiting [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] once it opens.

## Session 10

- Was forging an enormous sword when the party entered.
- Every hammer strike briefly wreathed his arm in flame from shoulder to hand.
- Introduced himself as Embric, with Avi as the armorsmith.
- Bought or accepted Aladeen's spear for 4 sp of credit on a Trollskull account.
- Noted that a bugbear like [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] is unusual outside the Xanathar context.
