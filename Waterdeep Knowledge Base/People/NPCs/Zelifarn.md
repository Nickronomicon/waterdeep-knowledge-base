# Zelifarn

Zelifarn is a young bronze dragon living around a wreck in [[Places/Deepwater Harbor]]. The party met him during the [[Factions/Gray Hands]] harbor assignment in [[Sessions/Session 12 - 2026-07-09]].

## Current Status

- Not hostile to Waterdeep as of the session 12 report.
- Curious about air-breathers, surface life, stories, and odd treasures.
- Likes thieves because they bring treasure to him when they try to steal from him.
- Has not sunk any ships since arriving, and the wreck he occupies predates him.
- Accepted a silver piece from [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] for his collection.
- Agreed to a friendly relationship with the party framed around visits, questions, and possible small treasures.
- Has no interest in dealing with mind flayers.

## Broethuul Connection

Zelifarn recognized [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] as smelling like a "wrong-water door." He said another door-smelling air-thing had recently approached the harbor, briefly passed through a door, and left [[Objects/The Amberleaf Dispatch]] for Broethuul.

Zelifarn delivered the leaf and considered it better than treasure, though Broethuul kept it and said he might return it after copying or reading it.

## Name Notes

The transcript has several phonetic spellings, including Zelopharn and Zelifern. Use Zelifarn as the stable page name unless the table later corrects it.
