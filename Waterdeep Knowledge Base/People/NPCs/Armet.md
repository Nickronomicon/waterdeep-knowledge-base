# Armet

Armet is a Dock Ward worker rescued from the grell in the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]] during [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]].

## Current Status

- Alive after Jonathan healed him.
- Grateful to the party and agreed that he had never seen them.
- Fled the scene before the Watch arrived.

## Known Facts

- Was sent by his boss to get the warehouse running on Ches 3.
- Remembered that someone disappeared two nights earlier.
- Woke up with the grell's tentacle in his stomach and the creature floating over him.
- Named his employer or boss as "Jonas and Co," which the party did not recognize.

## Open Questions

- Is Jonas and Co legitimate, a front, or a transcript rendering of another name?
