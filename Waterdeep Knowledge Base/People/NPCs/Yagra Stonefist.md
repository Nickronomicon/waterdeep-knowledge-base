# Yagra Stonefist

Yagra Stonefist is a half-orc mercenary whose fight with [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] helped introduce the party to the open Zhentarim/Xanathar tension in Waterdeep.

## Session History

- [[Sessions/Session 01 - 2026-02-15]]: Fought Krentz and his companions in the Yawning Portal. She dropped Krentz first but was knocked out by his allies before Durnan ended the fight.
