# Yagra Stonefist

Yagra Stonefist is a half-orc mercenary whose fight with [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] helped introduce the party to the open Zhentarim/Xanathar tension in Waterdeep.

## Session History

- [[Sessions/Session 01 - 2026-02-15]]: Fought Krentz and his companions in the Yawning Portal. She dropped Krentz first but was knocked out by his allies before Durnan ended the fight.
- [[Sessions/Session 08 - 2026-04-30]]: [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]] described driving a sun elf and half-orc/orc bodyguard to the [[Places/Yawning Portal]] while they discussed hiring spies to locate Xanathar Guild hideouts. The party connected the pair to [[People/NPCs/Davil Starsong]] and Yagra, suggesting Yagra is involved with the [[Factions/Zhentarim]] / Black Network side of the street war.

## Open Questions

- Is Yagra acting as Davil's bodyguard, partner, or operative?
- Does her conflict with [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] in session 1 connect directly to the spy network Maxeene overheard?
