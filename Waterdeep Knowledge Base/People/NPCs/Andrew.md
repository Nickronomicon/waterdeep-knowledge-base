# Andrew

Andrew is a clerk at the Guild of Scriveners, Scribes, and Clerks who helped Elior and Jonathan pull property records during [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]].

## Known Facts

- Helped locate the public record for the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]].
- Discovered that the warehouse ownership chain had been neatly redacted.
- Did not charge the normal two-silver record-pull fee because the record could not be rendered.
- Off the record, said redacted property records usually imply Closed Lord business, Xanathar influence, Zhentarim influence, or some combination.
- Was embarrassed by the missing record but lacked authority to access or restore it.
