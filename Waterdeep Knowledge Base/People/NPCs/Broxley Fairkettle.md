# Broxley Fairkettle

Broxley Fairkettle is a halfling representative of the [[Factions/Fellowship of Innkeepers]] who visited [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] in [[Sessions/Session 08 - 2026-04-30]].

## Session 8

- Arrived at Trollskull Manor after hearing that the party had taken over the old tavern.
- Was excited at the idea of reopening a venerable establishment, then visibly discouraged by the building's condition.
- Asked about the haunting; the party framed the residents as friendly or highborn supernatural presences.
- Explained that the Fellowship can provide rules, hours, guidelines, and operating expectations for a tavern or inn.
- Estimated guild dues at roughly 10 gp per tenday as part of regular business expenses.
- Warned that guild members may object to a non-guild tavern operating in Waterdeep.
- Said [[Places/Frewn's Brews]] is a guild member, though behind on dues, and implied Frewn's clientele should not be Trollskull Manor's model.
- Pointed to the [[Factions/Cassalanters]] as major moneylenders when the party discussed financing.

## Open Questions

- Has the party formally joined the Fellowship yet?
- What bylaws or operating rules will Broxley send?
- Will Broxley become an ally, inspector, or pressure point once Trollskull Manor nears opening?
