# Laraelra Harsard

Laraelra "Elra" Harsard is [[People/NPCs/Vajra Safahr|Vajra Safahr's]] contact at the [[Places/Drowned Lantern]] for the Deepwater Harbor bronze-dragon assignment in [[Sessions/Session 12 - 2026-07-09]].

## Current Status

- Met the party at the Drowned Lantern with potions of water breathing.
- Made clear that the mission was to investigate and report, not kill a dragon.
- Warned the party that a friendly dragon can still be dangerous.
- Did not intervene during the Xanathar-linked dock fight, later saying the possibility of spies and bribes was why the Watch had not been involved.
- Accepted the party's report that [[People/NPCs/Zelifarn]] was not hostile.
- Rewarded the party with five spare [[Objects/Force Grey Water Breathing Potions]].

## Table Notes

Elra is controlled, dry, and martial in presentation. She prefers that the party not advertise the Blackstaff's involvement and said it would be better if she never set foot in their tavern.
