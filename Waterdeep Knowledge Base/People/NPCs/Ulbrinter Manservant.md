# Ulbrinter Manservant

The Ulbrinter manservant is an unnamed elderly servant of [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] who delivered her calling card to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] and received the party at [[Places/Ulbrinter Villa]] in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]].

## Current Status

- Wears a coat with tails and a great deal of perfume.
- Delivered the Ulbrinter calling card requesting that the party visit "at your earliest convenience."
- Had a handkerchief with the Ulbrinter crest stolen by [[People/NPCs/Squiddly]] during the Trollskull visit.
- Later appeared at Ulbrinter Villa with a replacement handkerchief.
- Ushered the party into Remallia's morning room and maintained the household's old noble formality.
- Received Remallia's note to learn everything possible about [[People/NPCs/Thugby]] by the end of the day.
- Seems deeply devoted to Remallia; the party read his care as love that may be lifelong, unrequited, or unspoken.

## Open Questions

- What is his name?
- Is he a Harper agent, a household servant aware of Harper activity, or simply trusted by Remallia?
- What will his investigation discover about Thugby and the Thomril & Sons arson crew?
