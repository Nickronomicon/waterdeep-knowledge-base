# Xoblob

Xoblob is the deep gnome proprietor of [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]], a purple curiosity shop across from [[Places/Skewered Dragon|the Skewered Dragon]].

## Session 2

- Confirmed that two well-dressed men left the Skewered Dragon and were jumped by five rough men outside his shop.
- Saw dark cloaks, black leather armor, and one black hand tattoo close to Zhentarim markings.
- Pointed the party north, toward Candle Lane.
- Offered to buy one or both of Broethuul's purple eyes, pricing a right eye at 1 gp 18 sp.
- Sold Elior a purple tankard cozy for 5 sp.
