# Brother Senric

Brother Senric is a young Kelemvorite acolyte serving at [[Places/The Plinth]]. The party consulted him in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]] while looking for someone who could speak with the spirits at [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Current Status

- Second year at the Plinth and third year in service to Kelemvor.
- Young, eager, and clearly excited to have visitors interested in his altar.
- Received an aged mackerel from [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] as an awkward offering and a 1 sp donation from the party.
- Could not personally speak with ghosts or perform the kind of spiritual consultation the party wanted.
- Can likely help connect the party to a proper Kelemvorite exorcist if they decide to put Trollskull's spirits to rest.

## Beliefs Expressed

- Lingering souls should normally be helped onward rather than kept bound to the world.
- He framed ghosts as souls clinging to life in a potentially unhealthy or painful way.
- He suggested that if the party needed an exorcism, Kelemvor's clergy or possibly a Lathanderite could help.
- He reacted badly when [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] loudly asked about necromancy inside the temple.

## Impact on Party

- Senric's explanation seemed to move Elior toward treating the Trollskull haunting as a possible unresolved-soul problem rather than only a tavern feature.
- The party did not commit to an exorcism and instead continued looking for someone who could communicate with ghosts.
