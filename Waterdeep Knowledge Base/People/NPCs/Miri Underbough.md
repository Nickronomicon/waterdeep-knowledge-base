# Miri Underbough

Miri Underbough is a halfling cook or sous chef who came to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] with her brother [[People/NPCs/Corvin Underbough]] in [[Sessions/Session 10 - 2026-06-11]].

## Current Status

- Trial cook applicant at Trollskull Manor.
- More reserved than Corvin, with a practical but nervous manner.
- Encountered the kitchen haunting before dinner and was badly frightened.
- Has not yet completed the trial dinner in ingested transcript truth.

## Session 10

- Arrived with Corvin, bedrolls, and a covered basket.
- Reacted to the state of the pantry and kitchen but stayed with the trial.
- Saw pots and pans move in the kitchen, then fled behind the bar.
- Was present when the ghost wrote "I think we've got rats" in grime on the wall.
- [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] and [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] reassured her that the ghosts were helpful or at least manageable.
- Asked whether she could wait outside until Corvin returned.
