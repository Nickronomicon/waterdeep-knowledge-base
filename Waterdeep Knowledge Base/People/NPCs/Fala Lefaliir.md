# Fala Lefaliir

Fala Lefaliir is the wood elf herbalist who runs [[Places/Corellon's Crown]] in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]].

## Current Role

- Friendly neighbor to the party.
- Wants Trollskull Manor restored because the whole alley is named after it and benefits from its revival.
- Keeps aromatics, herbs, and potions.
- Confirmed in session 6 to have some magical ability, used practically and subtly.
- In session 9, [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] gave the party [[Objects/Remallia's Fala Credit Note]], which they can show Fala if they need medicine and cannot pay immediately.
- In session 11, [[People/NPCs/Lif]] told the party that Fala might know more about strengthening or understanding him, and asked them to tell Fala that Lif sends his regards.

## Session 4

- Greeted Aladeen warmly as one of Volo's friends and a new neighbor.
- Provided sage and a lavender, baby's breath, and thistle bundle to help with Trollskull Manor's smell.
- Gifted the aromatics freely.
- Said they would be one of the party's first customers when the manor reopens.

## Session 6

- Checked on the party after they returned from the Dock Ward covered in grell remains and noticed Elior was missing.
- Identified the residue as aberrant rather than ordinary beast or human blood.
- Shared Trollskull Manor history and ghost rumors with [[People/NPCs/Tally Fellbranch]].
- Saw the bar rag gesture from the haunting.
- Described the floating eye near Trollskull Manor as a gazer-like creature.
- Magically tailored and recolored Davy's gaudy opera outfit into a plausible blue formal look lasting about eight hours.
- Refused Davy's offered gold, saying the intervention was for the sake of the entire alley.

## Session 9

- Became part of Remallia's quiet support network for the party, at least for medicine or emergency supplies, through the credit note.
- Has not yet been shown the note in transcript truth.

## Session 11 Lead

- Lif named Fala as someone who might know more about his ghostly condition or whether he can be strengthened.
- Lif's message to Fala: he sends his regards.
- The party has not yet gone to Fala with this message in transcript truth.
