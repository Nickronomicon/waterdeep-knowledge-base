# Rishaal the Page-Turner

Rishaal the Page-Turner is the gold dragonborn bookseller who runs [[Places/Book Wyrm's Treasure]] in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]]. The transcript renders his name roughly as "Rochelle"; use Rishaal from the handout as canonical.

## Current Role

- Friendly neighbor and bookshop owner.
- Loaned Aladeen monster reference books as a welcome-to-the-street gesture.
- Invited magically inclined friends, especially Elior, to come examine the shop's scroll collection.
- Still owed the return of Aladeen's borrowed books as of session 10.

## Session 6

- Greeted Aladeen warmly as one of the new Trollskull Manor owners.
- Produced several books when Aladeen asked about grells, gazers, aberrations, and ghosts.
- Warned that Volo's monster writing should be taken with a large grain of salt.
- Loaned the books for free and asked that they be returned.
