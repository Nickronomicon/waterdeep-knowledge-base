Durnan is the proprietor of [[Places/Yawning Portal|the Yawning Portal]] and a central social anchor for the party's early Waterdeep life.

- [[Sessions/Session 02 - 2026-02-22]]: Mentioned in recap as part of the party's shared Yawning Portal orbit.
