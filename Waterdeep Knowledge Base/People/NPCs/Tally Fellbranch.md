# Tally Fellbranch

Talisolvanar "Tally" Fellbranch is the half-elf carpenter and woodcarver who runs [[Places/The Bent Nail]] in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]].

## Current Role

- Neighbor to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Practical, watchful, and concerned about danger spilling into the alley.
- Brought whiskey to Trollskull Manor after the party returned from the Dock Ward grell fight.
- Knows a long chain of Trollskull Manor history and ghost rumors.

## Session 6

- Asked the party whether anything followed them back, because he would rather know before it took an interest in his shutters.
- Shared Trollskull Manor history with [[People/NPCs/Fala Lefaliir]].
- Confirmed he had never met the old Mirthkettle "hag" figure; she was already a story when he arrived.
- Said [[People/NPCs/Lif]] died about forty years ago after a support beam on ale casks collapsed on him.
- Offered to keep an eye out after the party's clue items briefly went missing.
- Play-sword-fought his way through the urchins on his way back to his shop.
