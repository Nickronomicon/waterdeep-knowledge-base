# Grum'shar

Grum'shar was a half-orc spellcaster in the [[Places/Xanathar Sewer Hideout]]. The party found him standing over [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] with a fiery hand while [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]] prepared to leave.

## Session 3 Events

- Was torturing or threatening Floon when the party opened the hideout chamber.
- Asked how the party got in.
- Cast Burning Hands on Davy, Renaer, and Broethuul after Nihiloor escaped.
- Survived Broethuul's killing crossbow shot briefly through half-orc endurance.
- Was killed by Jonathan with a critical morningstar strike.
- Carried [[Objects/Grum'shar's Spellbook]] in a satchel.
