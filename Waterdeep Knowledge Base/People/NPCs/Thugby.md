# Thugby

Thugby is the table nickname for the surrendered hired thug from the Castle Ward ambush after [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]]. The transcript did not capture a real name.

## Session 8

- Claimed to be local hired muscle who worked around bars and had been hired to jump "a bunch of dandies."
- The party tried to turn him into a future informant or saboteur if he was hired against them again.
- Jonathan intimidated him effectively, and Thugby seemed to understand that crossing the party again was dangerous.
- Later the same session, Broethuul recognized Thugby among the arson crew attacking [[Places/Thomril & Sons Cartage]].
- Thugby recognized the party, froze with indecision, and failed to take meaningful action.
- When the fight turned against the attackers, Thugby fled.
- Another attacker shot at him as he ran, suggesting the crew did not appreciate desertion.

## Current Status

Escaped alive. He now knows the party recognizes him, and the party knows he immediately fell back in with people attacking one of their Harper contacts.

In session 9, the party told [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] about Thugby. Remallia wrote his name down and sent [[People/NPCs/Ulbrinter Manservant|her manservant]] to learn everything possible about him by the end of the day.

## Open Questions

- Did Thugby report back to whoever hired the arson crew?
- Does he know who ordered the Thomril & Sons attack?
- Is he frightened enough to be flipped later, or only frightened enough to avoid the party?
- What does Remallia's household learn from investigating him?
