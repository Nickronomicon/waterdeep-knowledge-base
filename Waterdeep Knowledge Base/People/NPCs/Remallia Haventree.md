# Remallia Haventree

Remallia Haventree, also known as the Widow Ulbrinter, is a secluded Waterdhavian noble, sun elf, and active [[Factions/Harpers|Harper]] contact. She appeared publicly at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]] and received the party at [[Places/Ulbrinter Villa]] in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]].

## Current Status

- Appeared publicly at the opera.
- Elior recognized that this was notable because she had not been seen in public for roughly five years after her husband's death.
- Known for continuing charitable and philanthropic work from her villa despite her seclusion.
- Invited the party to Ulbrinter Villa through [[Objects/Ulbrinter Calling Card]] after learning from [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] that they had spoken with [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]].
- Confirmed herself as the party's more day-to-day Harper handler while framing Mirt as a bigger-picture recruiter, financier, and city-level operator.
- Heard the Maxeene report, the Thomril & Sons attack report, and [[Objects/Scrap of Note]].
- Sent her manservant to investigate [[People/NPCs/Thugby]] by the end of the day.
- Gave the party [[Objects/Remallia's Fala Credit Note]], [[Objects/Remallia's Paper Bird]], and [[Objects/Remallia's Lender List]].
- Advised that the best thing the party could do for Waterdeep and the Harpers is get [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] open, because taverns gather information.
- Offered a cover story that she had hired the party to clear stirges or crows from her chimney.
- Asked the party to call her Remy rather than Lady Remy.

## Thalosmere Archive Context

The private [[Objects/Thalosmere Archive Report on Remallia Haventree]] revealed to [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] characterizes Remallia as discreet, restrained, hospitable, principled, and likely good, but secretive in a deliberate and consequential way.

## Session 9 Notes

- Requested exact words and observations from Maxeene's information instead of interpretation.
- Treated the Davil/Yagra identification as plausible but still conjecture, because it came through description and context.
- Had not been told about the Scrap of Note before the party raised it.
- Considered the mix of criminal factions, Stone language, and noble references as a possible sign of another [[Threads/Great Game]].
- Did not know "Lady B" immediately, or at least did not reveal a candidate.
- Could not be a visible financial backer for Trollskull but could quietly point the party toward lenders.

## Open Questions

- What will her manservant learn about Thugby?
- How much does she already know about the Stone, the eyes, and the noble side of the Great Game?
- What will she do with the Maxeene report and Scrap of Note?
- How will she react if the party uses or loses the paper bird?
