# Corvin Underbough

Corvin Underbough is a halfling cook who came to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] with his sister [[People/NPCs/Miri Underbough]] in [[Sessions/Session 10 - 2026-06-11]], hoping to be hired before the tavern opens.

## Current Status

- Trial cook applicant at Trollskull Manor.
- Arrived with a covered basket, traveling gear, and Miri.
- Heard that Trollskull Manor might reopen as a public house and came looking for work.
- Was given 1 gp by [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] to buy ingredients and prepare dinner as a test.
- Dinner has not yet occurred in ingested transcript truth; session 10 ended before the trial meal.

## Session 10

- Introduced himself as Corvin and Miri as his sister and sous chef.
- Took stock of Trollskull's kitchen and pantry and realized the pantry was a total loss.
- Stayed determined despite the ruined conditions.
- Left to buy ingredients while Miri remained at the manor and encountered the kitchen haunting.

## Name Notes

The transcript sometimes renders the surname as "Underbow" and the given name as "Corbin." Use Corvin Underbough from DM prep/session scaffold as canonical unless corrected.
