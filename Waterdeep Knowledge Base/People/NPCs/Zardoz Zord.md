# Zardoz Zord

Zardoz Zord is a flamboyant Luskan ship captain or entertainer associated with [[Places/Sea Maidens Fair]]. He appeared at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] with [[People/NPCs/Margo Verida]] in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]].

## Current Status

- Attended the opera with Margo Verida.
- Introduced himself to Elior and seemed to expect recognition.
- Said he was originally from Luskan and sails up and down the Sword Coast.
- Said three ships are enough for him and that he is in Waterdeep through the festival season.
- Invited attention toward the Sea Maidens Fair, promising the whole city would know when it opened.

## Table-Facing Impression

The transcript describes him as if a peacock had been polymorphed into a human: extremely flamboyant, richly dressed, with something like a naval or fancy ship-captain style.
