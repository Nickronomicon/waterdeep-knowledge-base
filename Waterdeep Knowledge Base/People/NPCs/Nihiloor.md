# Nihiloor

Nihiloor is a mind flayer encountered in the [[Places/Xanathar Sewer Hideout]] during [[Sessions/Session 03 - 2026-03-01]]. The party saw him in black robes, seated near [[People/NPCs/Grum'shar]], calmly petting an intellect devourer while Floon was being tortured.

## Current Status

- Escaped alive through a portal.
- Connected to [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] operations under the Dock Ward.
- Has or had access to an orb-like activator for an eye-marked portal pedestal.

## Session 3 Events

- Ignored Jonathan's attempt at conversation.
- Told Grum'shar, "You know what to do."
- Projected a psychic command that froze Jonathan and Renaer; Davy resisted it.
- Placed a sphere into the eye pedestal, opened a portal, stepped through, and removed the sphere from the far side before vanishing.

## Open Questions

- What is Nihiloor's exact role in the Xanathar Guild operation?
- Was he personally involved in extracting information from Floon, Renaer, or the locket clue?
