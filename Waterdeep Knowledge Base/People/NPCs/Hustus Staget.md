# Hustus Staget

Hustus Staget is a [[Factions/City Watch]] captain in the Dock Ward. The party met him at [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] after rescuing [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] and before descending into the sewers to find [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]].

## Current Role

- Captain of the Watch in charge of the Dock Ward.
- Well liked by Dock Ward merchants and shopkeepers who are not involved in criminal work.
- Took statements from the party after the Candle Lane warehouse fight.
- Handed out copies of the [[Reference/Waterdeep Code Legal]].
- Declined to send Watch patrols into the sewers while gang warfare was active on the streets.
- Gave the practical warning to keep blood off the streets.

## Table-Facing Impression

Staget is calm, dry, and professional rather than theatrical. He recognized Jonathan as Lord Eagleshield and reacted to the Thalosmere name, but still emphasized that noble status does not place the party above Waterdeep's law.
