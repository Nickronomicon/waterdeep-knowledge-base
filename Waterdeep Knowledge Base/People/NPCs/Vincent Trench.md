# Vincent Trench

Vincent Trench is the private investigator who runs [[Places/Tiger's Eye]] in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]].

## Current Role

- Discreet information broker/private eye.
- Can potentially investigate the Xanathar-linked gazer watching [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Has not yet been formally hired by the party.

## Session 6

- Met Aladeen after Aladeen rang the bell at Tiger's Eye.
- Was sharply dressed, pipe-smoking, and looked as if he had just woken up.
- Said that if someone sends a gazer around to look at the party, it always leads back to Xanathar.
- Clarified that he does not know which Xanathar operative is responsible, but could possibly find out for pay.
- Believes the gazer was watching the party, not the alley itself.
- Agreed to warn the party if he sees anything around the alley.
