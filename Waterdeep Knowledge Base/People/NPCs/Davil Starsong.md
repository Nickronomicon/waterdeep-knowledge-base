# Davil Starsong

Davil Starsong is a sun elf associated by table inference with the [[Factions/Zhentarim]] / Black Network. He has not yet appeared directly to the party in ingested transcript truth, but [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]] described giving a ride to a sun elf and half-orc/orc bodyguard whose details the party connected to Davil and [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]].

## Session 8 Information

- Maxeene drove a sun elf and Yagra-like bodyguard two days before the party interviewed her.
- She dropped them at [[Places/Yawning Portal]].
- They discussed hiring enough spies to root out [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] hideouts in the city.
- Their tone suggested factional rivals trying to make a mark, not Watch officers or civic do-gooders.
- The party connected the pair to the [[Factions/Zhentarim]].
