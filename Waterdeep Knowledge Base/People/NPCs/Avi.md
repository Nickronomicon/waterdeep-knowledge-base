# Avi

Avi is the water genasi armorsmith at [[Places/Steam and Steel]] and [[People/NPCs/Embric|Embric's]] husband.

## Current Role

- Runs Steam and Steel with Embric.
- Handles armor, shields, and armor-craft explanation.
- Carries standard metal armor and shields at guild prices.

## Session 10

- Was tending the forge when the party entered, working around a large shield.
- Has a bluish cast to his skin, with shifting water-like swirls visible when he moves.
- Enthusiastically explained shield and armor theory to [[People/PCs/Aladeen]].
- Recommended that children should train with gear close to what they would actually use, though real shields were more expensive than Aladeen wanted to spend.
- Showed Aladeen the handcrafted studs used to upgrade leather armor into studded leather.
