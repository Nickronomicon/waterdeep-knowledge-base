# Willifort Crowelle

Willifort Crowelle is the Cassalanter majordomo or senior house servant who delivered the [[Objects/Cassalanter Loan]] documents and gold to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] in [[Sessions/Session 12 - 2026-07-09]].

## Current Status

- Appeared at Trollskull Manor at midday on Ches 6, 1492 DR, with two porters and the 2,000 gp loan chest.
- Presented the paperwork on behalf of [[People/NPCs/Victoro Cassalanter]] and [[People/NPCs/Ammalia Cassalanter]].
- Had a polished, snobbish manner, especially around Trollskull's still-rough condition.
- Left after the party signed and the chest was opened.
