# Mr Jones

Mr. Jones was the apparent leader or employer of the ambush that followed the party's meeting with [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]].

## Current Status

- Dead.
- Met the fleeing watcher at a raised plaza in the Castle Ward back streets.
- Taunted the party about sitting in the wrong part of the story.
- Was badly wounded by Broethuul before acting.
- Was killed by Broethuul, then briefly animated by Broethuul's Fallen Puppet to strike the original watcher with his mace.

## Known Facts

- Hired mercenaries to jump "a bunch of dandies" when they showed up.
- Promised 40 gp per mercenary, payable after the job.
- Carried 13 gp, 6 sp, a simple mace, a heavy crossbow, poor leather armor, and [[Objects/Scrap of Note]].

## Open Questions

- Who did Mr. Jones work for?
- Was the name an alias?
- How did he know to watch Box C and the party?
