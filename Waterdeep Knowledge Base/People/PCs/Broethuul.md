# Broethuul

Broethuul is a level 3 bugbear Blood Hunter using the homebrew Order of the Faeslayer specialization. He is a displaced Faewild aristocrat: charming, lazy, dangerous with a rapier, and carrying unresolved claims tied to the Starfern clan and the Autumn Court.

## Combat Profile

- Primary attack: rapier +5, 1d8+5 piercing.
- Fighting style: Dueling.
- Crimson Rite: Rite of the Storm adds lightning damage while active, at the cost of necrotic self-damage when invoked.
- Blood Curse: Fallen Puppet.
- Blood Maledict: 1 use per short rest on the exported sheet; Curse Specialist adds another use and broadens valid targets.
- Important bugbear traits: long-limbed reach, darkvision, fey ancestry, powerful build, stealthiness, and surprise attack.

## Character Notes

- Male/presenting male, age 21, Medium, 6 ft. 8 in., 220 lb.
- Faith or cosmological tie listed as Fae Royalty.
- Personality: jovial, flirtatious, extremely lazy, aristocratic.
- Background feature: Position of Privilege.
- Broethuul belongs to the Starfern Fae clan, leaders of the Ao Shay Court and claimants to the Autumn Court.
- He was raised in the Haunted Aielwood of the Faewild and educated as hidden/exiled Fae nobility.
- At 16, his keep was attacked; he was pushed through a portal and emerged in Waterdeep's harbor near Umberlee's Cache.
- He has lived in Waterdeep for about five years, scraping by through charm, performance, troublemaking, and occasional crime.
- Threestrings of the Yawning Portal is a favorite humanoid contact because she reminds him of Fae court revelry.
