## Major Prep Threads To Track

- [[Places/Trollskull Manor|Trollskull Manor]] ownership, repair costs, staffing, haunting, and eventual opening.
- Urchins as local chorus and potential alarm system.
- Emmet Frewn as a petty local rival with later sabotage potential.
- Gray Hands/Force Grey as Vajra's official-but-irregular patronage track.
- Harpers as the subtler Renaer, Threestrings, Mirt, and Remallia track.
- Xanathar-linked monster releases as public panic or Watch-distraction assets.
- Zhentarim, Davil, Yagra, and Maxeene clues around the Grand Game.
- Cassalanter financing as both business solution and infernal/noble pressure vector.
- Underbough infiltration as tavern-staff opportunity and hidden sabotage risk.
- Stone of Golorr clue state, especially the note from the theater/ambush prep.
- Dorgar/Myrkul ghost-contact track and whether it collides with Kelemvorite or Lathanderite expectations.
- Zelifarn, Deepwater Harbor, and Gray Hands contact paths as a new dragon/faction surface.
- Broethuul's Amberleaf Dispatch as a private bridge between the Autumn Court, the Stone/Eye, the Crommor horn, and Cassalanter-adjacent contract imagery.

## Transcript Pass Checklist

- Create or finalize the session page.
- Record start state and end state.
- Split actual events from planned events.
- Update involved PCs, NPCs, places, factions, objects, open threads, and dashboards.
- Mark reveal status for handouts and private information.
- Add unresolved questions and next-session hooks.
- Update [[INDEX]], [[CHANGELOG]], and this map if the source pairing changes.
