# Floon Rescue

The party rescued [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] for [[People/NPCs/Volo]] in [[Sessions/Session 03 - 2026-03-01]]. The immediate rescue thread is resolved, though it exposed deeper [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] activity under the Dock Ward.

## Current State

- Volo paid 10 gp each up front.
- Volo promised 100 gp each on completion, but could not pay the cash reward.
- Volo instead offered the [[Objects/Trollskull Manor Deed]].
- Floon was abducted with [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] outside [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]].
- The [[Factions/Zhentarim]] took both men to [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]].
- Xanathar-linked attackers raided the warehouse and took Floon.
- The party followed yellow signs to the [[Places/Xanathar Sewer Hideout]].
- Floon was found under [[People/NPCs/Grum'shar|Grum'shar's]] foot, badly injured but alive.
- Aladeen used a healing potion to stabilize Floon before returning him to the [[Places/Yawning Portal]].

## Consequences

- [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]] escaped through a portal.
- [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] was seen deeper in the hideout, but the party chose not to pursue.
- The reward dispute became the Trollskull Manor ownership hook.
