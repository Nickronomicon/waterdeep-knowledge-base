# Cassalanter Warning in the Bugle

The ceremonial bugle from [[Threads/Crommor Incident]] contained [[Objects/The Crommor Warning|a warning meant for Laeral Silverhand]]. The warning concerned infernal contracts, a powerful house prospering too cleanly around Laeral's rise, and Waterdeep being pledged in hidden pieces.

## Working Truth

Davy's DM arc note states that the bugle carried a note detailing Cassalanter's pact with Asmodeus, meant for Laeral alone. The new unrevealed Davy handout gives the recovered warning text: it does not name House Cassalanter directly, but it describes infernal-contract patterns, abnormal prosperity, and civic-duty language that point at the Cassalanters.

House Thalosmere's archive independently classifies the bugle as a transmission vector and the event as a correction that preserved stability over truth.

Broethuul's memory adds a non-Thalosmere corroboration: the event felt binding, contractual, and not fey.

## Uses at the Table

- This can vindicate Davy without making the moment simple.
- It can implicate the Cassalanters before the party fully understands the Remix's broader villain structure, while preserving plausible deniability because the warning does not name them.
- It gives Elior a reason to pursue documentary, divinatory, or ledger evidence.
- It gives Laeral a reason to take personal interest without immediately solving the campaign.
