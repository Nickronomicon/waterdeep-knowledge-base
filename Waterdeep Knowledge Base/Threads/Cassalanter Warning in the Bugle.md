# Cassalanter Warning in the Bugle

The ceremonial bugle from [[Threads/Crommor Incident]] may have contained a warning meant for Laeral Silverhand. The warning concerned infernal contracts and a powerful house prospering too cleanly around Laeral's rise.

## Working Truth

Davy's DM arc note states that the bugle carried a note detailing Cassalanter's pact with Asmodeus, meant for Laeral alone. House Thalosmere's archive independently classifies the bugle as a transmission vector and the event as a correction that preserved stability over truth.

Broethuul's memory adds a non-Thalosmere corroboration: the event felt binding, contractual, and not fey.

## Uses at the Table

- This can vindicate Davy without making the moment simple.
- It can implicate the Cassalanters before the party fully understands the Remix's broader villain structure.
- It gives Elior a reason to pursue documentary, divinatory, or ledger evidence.
- It gives Laeral a reason to take personal interest without immediately solving the campaign.

## Open Questions

- Who wrote the warning?
- Was the original destroyed with the bugle?
- Does any copy exist in House Crommor, House Thalosmere, Cassalanter records, or Laeral's intelligence network?
- Did Victoro act to stop the message, seize the message, redirect blame, or cover for a third party?
