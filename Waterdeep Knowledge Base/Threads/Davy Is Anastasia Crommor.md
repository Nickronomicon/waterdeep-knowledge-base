# Davy Is Anastasia Crommor

[[People/PCs/Davy]] is Anastasia Crommor, a noble daughter of House Crommor living under a male street identity after the scandal that broke her family.

## Current Truth

- Anastasia left House Crommor after conflict with Davin Crommor.
- She survived on the streets as Davy, learned theft, and had run-ins with the Watch.
- Bonnie helped her with food at the Yawning Portal.
- Dorgar, a Myrkulite undertaker, gave Davy shelter and an apprenticeship in death-work.
- Davy has continued to associate with Aladeen and Threestrings without Aladeen recognizing her.

## Reveal Pressure

- Aladeen discovering Davy's identity reframes his most famous joke as harm done to a party member.
- Jonathan discovering Davy's identity revives the unfinished Eagleshield-Crommor alliance and his doubts about arranged injustice.
- Elior already registers Davy as persistently misfiled but does not yet know why.
- Laeral may know a warning existed but not who carried it.

## Prep Hooks

- A noble, priest, Watch officer, or old Yawning Portal contact recognizes Davy's habits, face, voice, or family tells.
- Dorgar asks Davy for help with a soul or burial that intersects with noble politics.
- Laeral asks Davy a carefully phrased question about the day Waterdeep cast someone out.
- A Crommor agent or creditor resurfaces, treating Davy as dead, missing, or useful.
