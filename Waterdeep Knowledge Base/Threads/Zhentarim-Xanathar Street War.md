# Zhentarim-Xanathar Street War

The party has stepped into an open conflict between the [[Factions/Zhentarim]] and the [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]]. By session 2, the conflict is visible in public streets, tavern rumors, and the Candle Lane warehouse aftermath.

## Current Evidence

- Session 1 introduced eye-tattooed [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] and his companions.
- Session 2 opened with [[Factions/City Watch]] processing a street skirmish with Zhentarim and Xanathar markings.
- Witnesses at [[Places/Skewered Dragon]] and [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]] saw possible Zhentarim abductors.
- [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] contained dead Zhentarim and Xanathar-linked bodies.
- [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] was likely taken when the Xanathar side raided the Zhentarim safe house.
- Session 3 confirmed a Xanathar sewer route marked by yellow circle-and-spoke signs.
- The party found [[Places/Xanathar Sewer Hideout]], killed several defenders, rescued Floon, and saw [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]] escape.
- [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] was seen alive deeper in the hideout with a deep gnome, apparently trying to barricade a door.

## Working Read

The factions are fighting over information connected to [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]], Dagult Neverember, and the rumored cache of dragons. Floon appears to have been swept up because he was mistaken for Renaer or used as leverage after the mistake became clear.
