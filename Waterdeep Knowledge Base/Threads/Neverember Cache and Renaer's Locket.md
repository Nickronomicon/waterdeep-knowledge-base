# Neverember Cache and Renaer's Locket

The session 2 rescue of [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] revealed that the gang conflict may be tied to rumors of Dagult Neverember's embezzled cache of dragons and to a hidden compartment in [[Objects/Renaer's Locket]].

## Current Evidence

- Zhentarim questioned Renaer about his father.
- Renaer says he has not spoken to Dagult in nearly a decade.
- Renaer admits embezzlement would be in character for Dagult, even if he personally knows nothing.
- The locket from Renaer's mother had a hidden compartment that Renaer did not know about.
- Whatever was in the compartment was gone by the time the party found the locket.
- A magical-looking sheet of parchment was found nearby.

## Open Questions

- What was hidden in the locket?
- Did the missing object point toward the cache?
- Did the Zhentarim find it, or did the Xanathar raid remove it?
- What is [[Objects/Magical Paper from Candle Lane Warehouse]]?
- How directly does this point toward the larger Dragon Heist?
