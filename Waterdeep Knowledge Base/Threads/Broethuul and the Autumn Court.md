# Broethuul and the Autumn Court

[[People/PCs/Broethuul]] is an exiled bugbear noble of the Starfern clan, which claims rightful rule over the Autumn Court. His court was discovered and attacked when he was sixteen, and he escaped through a portal into Waterdeep's harbor.

## Current Thread

Broethuul has lived in Waterdeep for five years without finding a way back through the portal near Umberlee's Cache. He wants, at least in theory, to gather power and return to avenge the Starfern massacre, but his laziness, love of revelry, and uncertainty keep the goal distant.

## Waterdeep Relevance

Broethuul sensed a contractual, non-fey distortion around the Crommor Incident, echoing the fate-currents near his own arrival. His fey story-logic gives the campaign a contrast point for Elior's divine ledger logic and the Cassalanters' infernal contract logic.

## Open Questions

- Who attacked the Starfern keep?
- Are agents of the hostile court searching for Broethuul in Faerun?
- Can Umberlee's Cache or another Waterdeep site reopen the route home?
- Does Broethuul's Order of the Faeslayer point toward his court's enemies?
