# Broethuul and the Autumn Court

[[People/PCs/Broethuul]] is an exiled bugbear noble of the Starfern clan, which claims rightful rule over the Autumn Court. His court was discovered and attacked when he was sixteen, and he escaped through a portal into Waterdeep's harbor.

## Current Thread

Broethuul has lived in Waterdeep for five years without finding a way back through the portal near Umberlee's Cache. He wants, at least in theory, to gather power and return to avenge the Starfern massacre, but his laziness, love of revelry, and uncertainty keep the goal distant.

In [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]], [[People/NPCs/Vajra Safahr]] publicly addressed him as Lord Broethuul and recognized his Autumn Court connection. Broethuul then told Elior that the wilds have two major courts, each with seven houses; his house was betrayed and attacked, likely by the Unseelie or Winter, and he escaped by swimming into Waterdeep's harbor.

In [[Sessions/Session 12 - 2026-07-09]], [[People/NPCs/Zelifarn]] delivered [[Objects/The Amberleaf Dispatch]] to Broethuul underwater in [[Places/Deepwater Harbor]]. The message was revealed privately to Broethuul and says the harbor threshold remembers him, frames him as Starfern, and links his claim to a memory-swallowing Stone/Eye and a lost Autumn compact.

## Waterdeep Relevance

Broethuul sensed a contractual, non-fey distortion around the Crommor Incident, echoing the fate-currents near his own arrival. His fey story-logic gives the campaign a contrast point for Elior's divine ledger logic and the Cassalanters' infernal contract logic.

The Amberleaf Dispatch makes that contrast more direct: it ties the broken Crommor horn, the "House of Coin and Smile," the Stone/Eye, and the lost Starfern claim into one private clue. This content is not party-wide knowledge unless Broethuul shares it.

## Open Questions

- Who attacked the Starfern keep?
- Are agents of the hostile court searching for Broethuul in Faerun?
- Can Umberlee's Cache or another Waterdeep site reopen the route home?
- Does Broethuul's Order of the Faeslayer point toward his court's enemies?
- Who sent the Amberleaf Dispatch, and can the wrong-water door in Deepwater Harbor be found or opened again?
- Does restoring swallowed memory make Broethuul a claimant or regent in Waterdeep's current political-fey knot?
