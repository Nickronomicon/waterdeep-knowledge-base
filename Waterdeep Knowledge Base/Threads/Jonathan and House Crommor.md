# Jonathan and House Crommor

[[People/PCs/Jonathan Usman Sonny Theodore Icarus Charles Eagleshield]] was once quietly positioned near House Crommor through a possible noble alliance with Anastasia Crommor. The alliance ended abruptly after the Crommor scandal.

## Current Tension

Jonathan was raised to embody justice and noble duty. Anastasia once asked him whether justice always required punishment, and he did not have an answer. Her disappearance and House Crommor's fast collapse felt arranged to him, even if he was told not to involve himself.

He does not yet appear to know that [[People/PCs/Davy]] is Anastasia.

## Prep Hooks

- Jonathan recognizes a detail in Davy's speech, posture, legal knowledge, or memory.
- Eagleshield family pressure resurfaces and frames the Crommors as unworthy.
- Jonathan is asked to punish someone before he knows the whole truth.
- Davy's reveal forces Jonathan to revisit whether obedience to family and city law served justice.
