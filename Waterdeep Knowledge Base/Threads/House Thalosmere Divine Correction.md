# House Thalosmere Divine Correction

House Thalosmere maintains records of a marginal divine function associated with correction: misassigned souls, contradictory prophecy, divine errors, and outcomes that resolve too neatly.

## Elior's Role

[[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] is the first undeniable evidence in generations that the function remains active. His scar operates like a living annotation: warming in response to misalignment, unresolved contradiction, and displaced truth.

## Party Signals

- Jonathan: properly filed divine power.
- Aladeen: narrative consequence redirected into performance.
- Davy: sustained warmth, as if placed in the wrong narrative space.
- Threestrings: controlled interference or narrative management.
- Bonnie: stable identity divergence.
- Broethuul: fey story-logic, not an error but a foreign filing system.

## Crommor Archive

House Thalosmere records classify the Crommor Incident as misassigned blame, interrupted transmission, and suspected infernal interference. The archive suggests the system preserved stability over truth.

## Session 9 Archive Use

- [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] received [[Objects/Thalosmere Archive Report on Remallia Haventree]] before meeting [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]].
- The report advised plain speech, restraint around grief, and careful attention to the room.
- Elior noticed the subtle Harper room-symbolism at [[Places/Ulbrinter Villa]] and later recognized Myrkul's symbol in the [[Places/Last Temple of Myrkul]].
- Elior's session 9 ghost-question arc now connects the correction thread to lingering souls: [[People/NPCs/Brother Senric]] argued for helping souls move onward, while [[People/NPCs/Dorgar]] offered a willingness-based communication rite.
