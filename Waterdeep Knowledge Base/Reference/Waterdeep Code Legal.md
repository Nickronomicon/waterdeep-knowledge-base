# Waterdeep Code Legal

The Code Legal is Waterdeep's legal handout. [[People/NPCs/Hustus Staget|Captain Hustus Staget]] gave copies to the party after the [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] fight in [[Sessions/Session 03 - 2026-03-01]].

## Campaign Use

- Staget used it as a warning that noble status does not exempt the party from Waterdeep law.
- He emphasized what the party could and could not do on the streets of the Dock Ward.
- His practical stance was that private trouble should stay private and blood should stay off the streets.
