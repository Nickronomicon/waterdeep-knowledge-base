# Remix Response Teams

Response teams are proactive nodes: they come looking for the party, contest heists, deliver offers, steal Golorr artifacts, retaliate, or create clues when the party is stuck.

## Deployment Principles

- Take five minutes during prep to decide what each active faction does next.
- Use response teams when the party makes noise, asks dangerous questions, crosses a faction, or stalls.
- Put clues on response teams: notes, visiting cards, playbills, passphrases, pass-amulets, or orders.
- A response team can be social, covert, or violent. It does not have to be a fight unless that is the cleanest pressure.

## Current Campaign Pressure

- [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] is the easiest immediate deployment because the party has damaged multiple Xanathar-linked assets and a harbor survivor may report back.
- [[Factions/Cassalanters]] should currently use soft leverage before open hostility: loan follow-up, helpful patronage, Willifort information-gathering, or social invitations.
- [[Factions/Harpers]] and [[Factions/Gray Hands]] are allied pressure rather than villain teams; use them to deliver leads, obligations, or consequences.
- [[Factions/Zhentarim]] response should preserve the table distinction between Davil/Yagra and Manshoonian violence unless a later source collapses that split.
