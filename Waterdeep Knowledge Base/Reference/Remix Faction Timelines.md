# Remix Faction Timelines

Structural timeline for the Remix's backstory and default spring timetable. Use this to understand faction knowledge and pressure; use [[Timeline/Campaign Timeline]] for actual table chronology.

## Backstory Spine

| Phase | Structural Event | Runtime Use |
|---|---|---|
| Neverember's embezzlement | Dagult Neverember hides the stolen dragons in the Melairkyn vault and uses the Stone to erase the vault's location from common knowledge. | Explains why the vault exists but its location is missing. |
| Stone blinded | Neverember removes the Stone's three Eyes and hides them separately. | Creates the Eye heist structure. |
| Neverember falls | Laeral replaces Neverember; his agents try to retrieve the Stone from the palace. | Starts the race from Waterdeep's political transition. |
| Xanathar gets the Stone | Xanathar agents steal the Stone from Neverember's agents. | Xanathar begins with the central artifact. |
| Cassalanters find one Eye | Their research leads them to the Brandath crypt. | Explains why the Cassalanters are already ahead in the Game. |
| Manshoon's Zhentarim steal one Eye | Agents in Neverwinter recover an Eye tied to Neverember's holdings. | Manshoon becomes a primary Grand Game player. |
| Zhent/Xanathar alliance fails | Zhentarim bring an Eye to bargain; Xanathar kills them and takes it. | Explains the street war and Xanathar's extra Eye. |
| Dalakhar steals the Stone | Neverember's agent infiltrates Xanathar and recovers the Stone. | Sets up the Fireball pivot. |
| Jarlaxle arrives | Sea Maidens Faire enters Waterdeep under Captain Zord cover. | Bregan D'aerthe can discover the Game through nimblewrights. |

## Default Remix Dates

The Remix's spring schedule assumes:

- Sea Maidens Faire arrives before the party becomes deeply involved.
- Trollskull's opening can land around Ches 19.
- Dalakhar steals the Stone around Ches 20.
- Fireball can strike around Ches 22.
- Fleetswake and Waukeentide provide social events, galas, parades, and heist cover.

## Current Campaign Fit

- Actual table chronology is earlier and slower than the Remix default: the party is on the morning of Ches 16 after session 12.
- Trollskull's intended opening window still lines up neatly with the Remix's suggested Ches 19 opening pressure.
- Fireball has not occurred. Keep the default Ches 20-22 Remix timing as a flexible pressure window, not a locked canon date.
- Zardoz and the Sea Maidens are already socially visible, while Cassalanter leverage is stronger than default because the party has signed the loan.

## Faction Knowledge Quick View

| Faction | Starts Knowing | Likely Wants Next |
|---|---|---|
| Xanathar | Stone significance, at least one Eye, Zhent threat | Recover the Stone and punish rivals. |
| Manshoonian Zhentarim | Eyes matter, Xanathar has or had the Stone, Neverember's Enigma is valuable | Hold or regain an Eye; locate Stone and other Eyes. |
| Cassalanters | Their Eye is part of Golorr; Neverember's Enigma can solve their money problem | Use the party or agents to assemble the Stone and claim the vault. |
| Bregan D'aerthe | Initially not in the Game | Discover the Game through nimblewright records, Zelifarn/harbor activity, or party contact. |
| Gralhunds | Enough to meddle, not enough to survive cleanly | Use the Stone for advancement or leverage. |
