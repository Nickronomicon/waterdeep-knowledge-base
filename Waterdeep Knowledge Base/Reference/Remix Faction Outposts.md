# Remix Faction Outposts

Faction outposts are flexible intermediate nodes: places where the party can investigate factions, rescue allies, intercept operations, find portable clues, or discover how to reach lairs.

## How To Use Outposts

- Point general faction research toward an outpost first.
- Use outposts as bases for operations that target the party or other factions.
- Let outpost clues lead to lairs, other outposts, or response teams.
- Reincorporate outposts early: a shopping trip, guild errand, or casual rumor can make a location feel natural before it becomes a Grand Game node.

## Current Campaign Hooks

- [[Places/Trollskull Smuggler's Passage]] can plausibly connect to Xanathar sewer activity, a second sewer hideout, or a response-team route.
- [[Factions/Cassalanters]] already have a signed loan relationship, so exposing the Asmodean Shrine is a strong mid-Game reversal if the party trusts them.
- [[Places/Sea Maidens Fair]] and [[People/NPCs/Zardoz Zord]] are already known socially; a nimblewright or Zelifarn lead can naturally convert that into a Bregan outpost/heist path.
- [[Factions/Zhentarim]] remains split at the table; be explicit whether an outpost belongs to Manshoon's faction, Doom Raider circles, or table-custom operators.
