# Remix Golorr Artifacts

Reference for the Remix's treatment of the Stone and Eyes. Use [[Objects/Stone of Golorr]] for campaign-facing status.

## Artifact Model

- The Stone is an abolethic artifact tied to erased knowledge and alien memory.
- Neverember used it to hide the vault's location, then blinded it by removing three Eyes.
- The Eyes are physical components that can be popped into or out of the Stone by someone attuned to it.
- The Stone's missing Eyes create the main heist loop.

## Blinded Stone States

| State | Runtime Meaning |
|---|---|
| No Eyes | Inert. It cannot meaningfully communicate or reveal the vault. |
| One Eye | It can communicate, but cannot yet reveal the hidden secrets. |
| Two Eyes | It can perceive the remaining Eye and its immediate surroundings. This can rescue the investigation if the party misses a lead. |
| Three Eyes | Fully restored and able to operate normally, including revealing the vault location through its secrets. |

## DM Use

- Make physical inspection matter: missing sockets should be visible once the Stone is examined.
- Let the Stone communicate cryptically and unpleasantly once partially restored.
- Treat the Stone as a source of clues, not a vending machine for answers.
- Remember that erased knowledge can leave negative space: records, rumors, or rituals may show where information was removed without restoring the missing fact.

## Current Campaign Touchpoints

- [[Objects/Scrap of Note]] has already used the language "Stone", "Blinded", and "eyes."
- [[Objects/Renaer's Locket]] is structurally important because the Remix ties it to one of the Eyes.
- Broethuul's [[Objects/The Amberleaf Dispatch]] is custom campaign material; it may become a parallel clue layer, but do not assume the Remix explains it directly.
