# Remix Revelation Lists

DM-facing clue structure for the major Remix mysteries. This page tracks what the party needs to learn, not exactly where every clue must appear.

## Stone of Golorr Revelations

| Revelation | Why It Matters | Candidate Clue Vectors |
|---|---|---|
| Dalakhar carried the Stone. | Makes Fireball/Nimblewright matter beyond a street crime. | Speak with dead, Grand Game NPC interrogation, nimblewright records, Gralhund material. |
| The Stone finds Neverember's Enigma. | Tells the party what the chase is actually about. | Research, faction reports, knowledgeable faction lieutenants. |
| The Stone is blinded. | Explains why the Stone alone is insufficient. | Physical examination, [[Objects/Scrap of Note]], faction reports, studying the Stone. |

## Three Eyes Revelations

| Revelation | Current Structural Answer | Candidate Clue Vectors |
|---|---|---|
| There are three missing Eyes. | The Stone was deliberately split by Neverember. | Study the Stone; Gralhund, Jarlaxle, Cassalanter, Xanathar, or Manshoon reports. |

| Xanathar Eye. | Held in Xanathar's lair. | Gralhund/Jarlaxle/Cassalanter/Manshoon reports. |
| Zhentarim Eye. | Held by Manshoon's Zhentarim at Kolat Towers. | Gralhund/Jarlaxle/Xanathar reports; Samara in Xanathar's lair. |

## Vault Key Revelations

The Remix preserves a separate step for learning the Vault keys after the Stone is restored or the entrance is found.

Candidate vectors:

- Examining the vault entrance.
- Cassalanter Grand Game report.
- Vision granted by the restored Stone.
- Kalain / Dalakhar backtracking material.

## Outpost And Lair Clue Logic

Use the Three Clue Rule by default:

- A faction outpost should usually contain clues to that faction's lair.
- A competing faction should have at least one clue to another faction's outpost or lair.
- Response teams should carry portable clues: notes, playbills, passes, visiting cards, passphrases, or captured intelligence.
- Interrogating field operatives usually points back to their outpost; interrogating outpost personnel can point toward lairs.

See [[Reference/Remix Faction Outposts]] and [[Reference/Remix Response Teams]] for the concrete menu.
