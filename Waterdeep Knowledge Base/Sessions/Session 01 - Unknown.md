# Session 01 - Unknown

Session 1 has no transcript in `../raw/`. This draft is reconstructed from the Discord synopsis and the DM's opening-scene prep.

## Summary

The party began at the Yawning Portal while Volothamp Geddarm finished a tavern tale. A fight broke out between Yagra Stonefist and Krentz, a bald thug with eye tattoos, backed by four companions. Yagra dropped Krentz first but was then knocked out by the others before Durnan intervened and ejected the thugs.

The commotion was interrupted by a troll climbing from the well with stirges. Durnan took the troll while the nearby party dealt with stirges and helped end the threat with oil and fire. Aladeen hid behind the bar and mocked the troll, while Jonathan helped with the oil-lamp response.

Afterward, Volo hired the party to find Floon Blagmaar, offering 10 gp up front and 100 gp each on completion. Floon was last seen at the Skewered Dragon in the Dock Ward, wearing a gaudy unicorn-head necklace on blue pearls.

## Important People

- Volo gave the job.
- Durnan defended the Yawning Portal from the troll.
- Yagra Stonefist fought Krentz and his companions.
- Krentz and the tattooed thugs established the early Xanathar pressure.
- Floon Blagmaar became the missing-person objective.

## End State

- The party accepted Volo's job and set out toward the Dock Ward with road flagons.
- Floon was missing and last tied to the Skewered Dragon.
- The party had already been exposed to Xanathar-coded street violence.
