# Session 02 - 2026-02-22

The party followed [[People/NPCs/Volo|Volo's]] lead from [[Places/Yawning Portal|the Yawning Portal]] into [[Places/Dock Ward|the Dock Ward]], confirmed that [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] and another noble had been abducted, found Floon's broken necklace trail, fought kenku in a [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse|Zhentarim warehouse on Candle Lane]], and rescued [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]]. The session ended with boots approaching the warehouse.

## Start State

- The party had just accepted Volo's job to find Floon.
- Volo had paid 10 gp each up front and promised 100 gp each on completion.
- Floon was last seen at [[Places/Skewered Dragon|the Skewered Dragon]] wearing [[Objects/Floon's Unicorn Necklace]].
- The party took a walking short rest while leaving the Yawning Portal with road flagons.
- Time of day: evening, with the sun already down or close to it.

## Major Scenes

### Street Violence On The Way

The party took major streets toward the Dock Ward, including Soothsayer's Way and the Way of the Dragon. They found a side street cordoned off by the [[Factions/City Watch]] with about half a dozen bodies and three bloody prisoners.

Elior asked whether any of the bodies wore a giant unicorn medallion with blue pearls. The Watch had not seen one. Elior then attempted to bribe a Watch officer with 1 gp; another officer knocked the coin away, and Jonathan's response made the moment more awkward. The party left before the interaction escalated.

The bodies showed two faction marks: black flying-snake tattoos associated with the [[Factions/Zhentarim]], and eye/starburst tattoos associated with the [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]]. The party understood that the local underworld conflict had begun spilling openly into the streets.

### First Table-Facing PC Introductions

On the walk, the table paused for fuller character introductions.

- [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] is a Waterdeep street-raised bard known for the Crommor Collapse routine.
- [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] presents as a young noble-adjacent human man in somewhat ornate but not top-tier clothing.
- [[People/PCs/Jonathan Usman Sonny Theodore Icarus Charles Eagleshield]] is a handsome aasimar noble paladin with starry eyes and a public reputation as House Eagleshield's proof of celestial favor.
- [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] is a slender, purple-eyed bugbear in threadbare, salt-stained finery of no recognizable Waterdhavian fashion.
- [[People/PCs/Davy]] appears as a willowy, self-cut, dark-haired teen boy, private and easy to overlook, with clean hygiene but a street-grimy look.

The party's shared social origin was also clarified: they were linked through [[People/NPCs/Bonnie]], [[People/NPCs/Threestrings]], and [[People/NPCs/Durnan]] at the Yawning Portal.

### The Skewered Dragon

The party reached the Skewered Dragon, a rough tavern near [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]]. The tavern was physically battered, with smashed windows and a ship's anchor lodged in the roof. The bartender was mute, scarred, missing fingers, and likely had a peg leg.

Aladeen bought a refill and asked after Floon's unicorn necklace. The bartender pointed him to a table of regular dockworkers. Elior bought a jug of rum for 10 silver and 10 copper, then shared it with the dockworkers to get them talking.

The dockworkers remembered Floon as a drunken, boisterous rich man who did not belong there. Floon had been drinking with Volo earlier, then another well-dressed noble arrived. Floon and the other noble left together shortly before Midnight Bell. Four or five non-regular roughs followed soon after; one had a black snake tattoo on his hand.

Davy eavesdropped around the room and heard that warehouses on Candle Lane had become more dangerous and that about half of them had shut down.

Broethuul tried to start a bar fight by stealing coins from a three-dragon-ante table and blaming someone else. Aladeen slipped away from the center of the conflict, Elior used Gust to scatter coins away from the party's table, Jonathan talked down the immediate violence, and Davy faded back. The moment became a scramble for loose coins rather than a full fight. Elior left the remaining rum with the dockworkers.

### Old Xoblob Shop

The party crossed to Old Xoblob Shop, a purple curiosity shop filled with dyed-purple oddities, lavender smoke, a plush beholder in the window, and [[People/NPCs/Xoblob]], a deep gnome with purple eye markings painted on his cheeks.

Xoblob confirmed that two well-dressed men had left the Skewered Dragon the previous night and were jumped almost immediately outside the shop by five rough men in dark cloaks and black leather armor. He saw one black hand tattoo that might have been Zhentarim. He pointed the party north.

Xoblob also became fascinated by Broethuul's purple eyes and offered 1 gp 18 sp for one right eye. Elior bought a purple tankard cozy for 5 sp.

### Floon's Necklace Trail

Outside the shop, Davy and Broethuul noticed the broken remains of Floon's unicorn necklace in the gutter: the gaudy unicorn medallion and only some of the blue pearls. The party found a trail of loose pearls leading north toward Candle Lane.

The trail led to a two-story warehouse with a black winged-snake mark on the door, a fenced yard, and a dead flying snake with an arrow through it. The warehouse looked abandoned or neglected from outside.

### Candle Lane Warehouse Fight

The party debated a loud entertainer-troupe approach versus stealth. One PC used disguise magic before the approach, but Jonathan ultimately kicked in the warehouse doors and demanded that evildoers show themselves.

Four arrows answered from hidden kenku. Jonathan dropped, then Aladeen used Healing Word to bring him back up. Davy shot from cover. Elior used Ice Knife and later Sorcerous Burst, including the now-noted yellow/gold snow visual. Broethuul sniped through the fence and dropped a kenku. Jonathan healed Elior after Elior dropped, tried to stop another fleeing kenku, threw his morningstar unsuccessfully, and used Compelled Duel. Davy finished a wounded fleeing kenku with a crossbow shot.

By the end of the fight, three kenku were dead, one kenku was dead in the street, and one kenku survived inside, panicked and disarmed.

### Kenku Clues

The party cornered the surviving kenku. It was nearly catatonic and repeated phrases in different copied voices:

- Xanathar sends his regards.
- Tie up the pretty boy in the back room.
- Follow the yellow signs in the sewers.
- No time to loot the place; get him to the boss.

The party debated whether to tie up, release, or knock out the kenku. Aladeen slapped it ineffectively while trying to knock it unconscious. When space opened, the kenku fled.

### Renaer Found

The warehouse contained many bodies, broken crates, old pickled-herring remnants, and signs of safe-house use rather than active commercial use. The bodies included five humans in black leather armor with black winged-snake tattoos and seven other humans in leather armor, one of whom had an eye/starburst tattoo on his palm.

Davy checked the storage room under the stairs. Elior pulled away a tarp and found [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]], alive but exhausted and smelling of sour fish and vinegar. Renaer recognized the Thalosmere and Eagleshield names, then asked Davy whether the name was short for Davin; Davy answered that it was just Davy.

Renaer explained that he came to retrieve Floon after hearing Floon was in a state from drinking with Volo. Zhentarim abducted both men, likely mistaking Floon for Renaer or Renaer for Floon. The Zhents questioned Renaer about his father, Dagult Neverember, and the rumored embezzled cache of dragons. A second group, likely Xanathar Guild, attacked the warehouse, killed the Zhents, searched quickly, and took Floon.

Renaer blamed himself and his father for Floon's danger and offered to help search for him.

### Upstairs Search

Upstairs, the party found an overseer's office, a steel alarm bell wired elsewhere in the building, a chair with rope bonds, and [[Objects/Renaer's Locket|Renaer's smashed locket]]. The locket contained a portrait of Renaer's mother and a hidden compartment whose contents were gone. Elior used Mending to repair the locket and returned it to Renaer, who was visibly moved.

The party also found [[Objects/Magical Paper from Candle Lane Warehouse|a magical-looking sheet of parchment]] under or near where the locket had been. A low Arcana result only established that it gave off magical vibes.

The final warehouse search suggested the building was a safe house or place to lie low, not a place where criminal business was generated. The session ended when the party heard hobnailed boots approaching the warehouse.

## Clues Gained

- Floon was very drunk at the Skewered Dragon the previous night.
- A second noble, likely Renaer Neverember, left with Floon.
- Four or five rough men followed them, one with a black snake tattoo.
- Candle Lane warehouses had become dangerous, with many shutting down.
- Floon's necklace broke outside Old Xoblob Shop and left a blue-pearl trail to the Candle Lane warehouse.
- The warehouse was marked with a black winged snake and contained dead Zhentarim and Xanathar-linked bodies.
- Kenku repeated a sewer clue: follow yellow signs in the sewers.
- Floon was likely taken by Xanathar Guild operatives after the Zhentarim abducted him by mistake.
- Renaer's locket had a hidden compartment, and whatever it held was missing.
- A magical-looking parchment was found upstairs.

## Combat And Consequences

- Jonathan was dropped by the opening kenku ambush and revived by Aladeen's Healing Word.
- Elior was dropped by a later arrow and revived by Jonathan's Cure Wounds.
- Davy was hit by an arrow, then killed the kenku who shot him.
- Broethuul killed a kenku with a crossbow shot through the fence.
- Three warehouse kenku and one fleeing street kenku died; one kenku escaped in panic.
- The party took a short rest while searching the warehouse at the end of the session.
