# Session 01 - 2026-02-15

Session 1 took place on Sunday, February 15, 2026, before the campaign switched to Thursday nights. It has no transcript in `../raw/`; this record is reconstructed from the Discord synopsis, the DM's opening-scene prep, and the session 2 recap.

## Summary

The party began at [[Places/Yawning Portal|the Yawning Portal]] while [[People/NPCs/Volo|Volo]] finished a tavern tale. A fight broke out between [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]] and [[People/NPCs/Krentz]], a bald thug with eye tattoos, backed by four companions. Yagra dropped Krentz first but was then knocked out by the others before [[People/NPCs/Durnan]] intervened and ejected the thugs.

The commotion was interrupted by a troll climbing from the well with stirges. Durnan took the troll while the nearby party dealt with stirges and helped end the threat with oil and fire. [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] hid behind the bar and mocked the troll, while [[People/PCs/Jonathan Usman Sonny Theodore Icarus Charles Eagleshield]] helped with the oil-lamp response.

Afterward, Volo hired the party to find [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]], offering 10 gp each up front and 100 gp each on completion. Floon was last seen at [[Places/Skewered Dragon|the Skewered Dragon]] in [[Places/Dock Ward|the Dock Ward]], wearing [[Objects/Floon's Unicorn Necklace|a gaudy unicorn-head necklace on blue pearls]].
