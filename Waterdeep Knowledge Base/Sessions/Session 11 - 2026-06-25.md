# Session 11 - 2026-06-25

Session 11 continued the evening of Ches 5, 1492 DR, at [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] immediately after the kitchen ghost wrote "I think we've got rats." The session covered the [[People/NPCs/Corvin Underbough|Corvin]] and [[People/NPCs/Miri Underbough|Miri Underbough]] trial dinner, a rat incursion from a hidden sewer passage, and [[People/NPCs/Dorgar|Dorgar's]] seance, which confirmed the manor's ghost as [[People/NPCs/Lif]].

The session ended that same night after Lif's message of welcome. The Cassalanter loan signing, guild payments, Tally's bucklers, and any next-day business management remain pending for the next session.

## Starting State

- The party was at Trollskull Manor on the evening of Ches 5.
- [[Objects/Dorgar's Seance Candles]] were nearly complete; the table clarified that Davy had likely started them after returning from Dorgar rather than the following morning, so the seance could occur later this same evening.
- The [[Objects/Cassalanter Loan]] was negotiated but still unsigned, with documents and funds expected the next day.
- The Underboughs were preparing their trial dinner in Trollskull's ruined kitchen and pantry.
- The kitchen ghost had warned, "I think we've got rats."

## Dorgar Arrives

[[People/NPCs/Dorgar]] arrived before dinner, darkening the doorway with his usual unsettling presence. [[People/PCs/Davy]] and the party brought him upstairs so he could prepare for the seance while dinner continued below.

- Dorgar's visible tools looked more like hedge-witch or folk-ritual supplies than overtly sinister necromancy: dried herbs, a sharp knife, twine, salt, and similar implements.
- He clarified that the candle preparation creates an opening that lasts up to three days, though the first day is strongest.
- The final candle had a couple of hours left to burn.
- [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] tried speaking to the ghost again before dinner, but received no answer.

## Underbough Trial Dinner

Corvin and Miri produced an ambitious meal despite Trollskull's damaged kitchen, ruined pantry, and limited resources.

- Gurdats: pan-fried mushrooms and cheese, prepared extremely well. Jonathan found the crispy cheese startlingly loud, but the dish impressed the party.
- Tahllap noodles: hot noodles with fresh vegetables, goat cheese, and a tangy sauce. Solid street-food tavern fare rather than upscale cooking.
- Tarnsar: a dwarven-style roasted boar haunch with potatoes, onions, herbs, glaze, and a little sweetness. The dish was excellent and demonstrated Corvin's skill as an event or festival cook.
- Harvelveauplum bread pudding: a halfling specialty with sweet-spicy plum, hot pepper, sweet pepper, and thello-fruit sauce. The dessert was overdone after the rat interruption, and everything still tasted a little like rat by then.

During the meal, the party asked why the Underboughs had left their previous work. Corvin said their last establishment, the Goose's Fat, closed through mismanagement and was later destroyed. Davy knew the restaurant had burned down a few days after closing rather than being demolished. No table-confirmed proof established whether the Underboughs had any connection to the fire.

Insight checks showed Corvin badly needed the job and was worried the trial would fail.

## Rats in the Cellar

Miri called for help from the cellar. Broethuul investigated and found her backing away from enormous rats. As the party descended, they discovered two giant rats and several swarms of normal rats flooding out of a hidden wall section.

- The rats emerged through a secret door in the cellar wall.
- [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] used Sleep to incapacitate several swarms, then followed with Vicious Mockery and Healing Word.
- Broethuul activated a moonlit blood-hunter rite on his rapier, causing nearby creatures to shimmer as if under Faerie Fire, and skewered one giant rat.
- [[People/PCs/Davy]] shot and stabbed rats, then was swarmed near the end of the fight.
- [[People/PCs/Jonathan Usman Sonny Theodore Icarus Charles Eagleshield|Jonathan]] fought in full armor and used Toll the Dead to kill swaths of rats clinging to him.
- [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] was absent as a player but active in fiction; he screamed when swarmed, repeatedly landed Sorcerous Burst, took a hard bite, and finished the last rats on Davy with a green mist-like spell effect.
- Miri fled outside, while Corvin positioned himself between his sister and the fight but did not rush into the cellar.

The party killed the rats without the fight becoming as dangerous as the Thomril & Sons fire, though Elior was hurt significantly.

## Smuggler's Passage

After the fight, Jonathan investigated the secret cellar door and found a narrow earthen passage leading to another hidden door. Davy forced the second door open with thieves' tools, revealing a connection to the city sewer.

- The first secret door leads from Trollskull's cellar into a concealed passage.
- The second secret door opens into a sewer tunnel running under the road near Trollskull Manor.
- The sewer passage is large enough for Small creatures and smaller to move through, and it had only a thin trickle of sludge and water.
- The sewer-side door had been closed and latched when the party reached it.
- The passage showed evidence of many rats moving through it, but no other obvious entrance.
- The party inferred that someone or something likely opened the passage and sent the rats into Trollskull, then closed the outer door again.
- The party blocked the cellar-side secret door with junk and old barrels as a noisy temporary measure.

Lif later confirmed the passage as an old smuggler's route to the sewers, mostly blocked off and unused since before his time.

## Underbough Decision Deferred

Corvin offered to stay and clean the kitchen after dinner, but the party asked the Underboughs to return the next morning for an answer.

- Corvin and Miri left cautiously optimistic.
- Corvin clearly disliked leaving the kitchen dirty.
- Miri was frightened by the rats but returned once the danger passed.
- The party has not formally hired them in transcript truth.

## Dorgar's Seance

After the Underboughs left, Dorgar cleared a ten-foot space in the taproom and prepared the rite.

- He poured a salt circle and worked it into a complex pattern with a sharp, stained wand-like tool.
- The pattern was difficult to look at directly, as if the mind slipped off it.
- The burnt stubs of the seance candles went at the center.
- Davy assisted as Dorgar's apprentice.
- Broethuul helped effectively with a strong arcane sense for the rite.
- Aladeen offered folklore-informed suggestions that Dorgar politely acknowledged and ignored.
- Jonathan and Elior observed skeptically.
- Dorgar warned that the spirit might speak directly, speak through him, or not come at all.
- The party had five questions if contact succeeded, and the spirit was not compelled to answer honestly.

The ritual dimmed every light in the room except the rising salt-circle glow. The air smelled sweet and floral, with faint distant funeral bells. A faint voice then spoke from the center of the circle and called the party the new wardens of Trollskull Manor.

## Lif's Answers

The responding spirit identified himself through context and later by name as Lif.

The party's questions and Lif's answers:

- What does he want for the manor? Restoration: the former glory of his home.
- How can he aid the party in that project? He can still serve drinks, clean, and wield a broom, but to his shame he can no longer greet guests.
- Are there other spirits inhabiting the manor? No; Lif said it is just him and that he is alone.
- What secrets are in the manor for the party to discover? Trollskull Manor is haunted, and there is a smuggler's passage to the sewers that has been mostly blocked off for years and unused since before Lif's time.
- Can the party strengthen him? Lif does not know; he was never a priest or magician. He suggested [[People/NPCs/Fala Lefaliir]] might know more and asked the party to tell Fala that Lif sends his regards.

When the fifth answer ended, the lights returned, the salt circle collapsed back into ordinary salt, and the word "welcome" appeared in grime behind the bar.

## End State

- It is still the night of Ches 5, 1492 DR.
- Lif is confirmed as the only Trollskull spirit currently present and wants the manor restored.
- Lif is willing and able to help in limited tavern-ghost ways: cleaning, broom-work, serving drinks, and likely written warnings.
- Fala Lefaliir is the next lead for learning whether Lif can be strengthened or better understood.
- The old Trollskull smuggler's passage to the sewers has been discovered and temporarily blocked from the cellar side.
- Someone or something may have used the passage to send rats into the manor.
- Corvin and Miri Underbough have completed the trial dinner but have not yet been formally hired.
- The Cassalanter loan signing, funds delivery, guild representatives, Tally's bucklers, and business-management decisions remain pending for the next morning or midday.
