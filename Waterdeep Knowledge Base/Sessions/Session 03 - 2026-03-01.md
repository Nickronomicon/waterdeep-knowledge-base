# Session 03 - 2026-03-01

Session 3 resolved the immediate [[Threads/Floon Rescue|Floon rescue]], starting late on Ches 1 and ending in the early morning hours of Ches 2, 1492 DR. The party dealt with the [[Factions/City Watch]] at [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]], followed yellow [[Factions/Xanathar Guild|Xanathar]] signs into the sewers, fought through a sewer hideout, rescued [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]], and returned him to [[People/NPCs/Volo]]. Volo could not pay the promised reward in coin and instead offered the deed to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Start State

- The party began inside [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] after finding [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] but not Floon.
- A captured kenku had pointed them toward yellow signs in the sewers.
- Hobnailed boots were approaching from outside the warehouse.

## Major Scenes

### City Watch At Candle Lane

- Renaer went outside to meet the approaching Watch and spoke with [[People/NPCs/Hustus Staget|Captain Hustus Staget]].
- Elior introduced himself as Elior Thalosmere and tried to frame the party as cooperative rescuers.
- Jonathan was recognized by Staget as Lord Eagleshield.
- Davy hid in the fish closet and was not included in the initial statement. Jonathan later called for Davy; Staget noticed the emergence but accepted the party's explanation.
- Staget took statements, accepted that the warehouse scene was gang-on-gang violence, and did not treat the party as suspects.
- Staget handed the party copies of the [[Reference/Waterdeep Code Legal]], stressing what they could and could not do on the streets of the Dock Ward.
- Staget said the Watch could not chase Floon into the sewers while open gang warfare continued above ground. His practical advice was to keep blood off the streets.

### Finding The Sewer Trail

- Davy found the nearest sewer entrance north of the warehouse.
- Yellow paint, like fingerprints, marked the edge of the manhole cover.
- The party debated elaborate alternatives, including finding a sewer-guild guide or disguising themselves as Xanathar operatives, but Renaer pushed to go after Floon immediately.
- Renaer acquired three healing potions from Staget before descending.
- Marching order into the sewer: Renaer, Aladeen, Jonathan, Broethuul, Elior, Davy.

### Gazer Lookout

- The party followed small yellow circle-and-spoke symbols through the reeking sewer tunnels for about an hour.
- At a three-way intersection, they encountered a floating gazer acting as a lookout near another yellow sign.
- Renaer stabbed it with dagger and rapier.
- Aladeen damaged its dignity with Vicious Mockery.
- Elior destroyed it with a golden, glyph-shedding Sorcerous Burst.
- The party briefly checked an upper access point and found it opened into a cellar or tavern/kitchen storage area, but did not investigate further.

### Arrow Slit Ambush

- The sewer path widened into a circular hub with arrow slits covering the approach.
- Renaer and several PCs tried to move quietly. Elior tripped and splashed into the muck, waking the sentries.
- Goblins shot from behind arrow slits. Elior was dropped by an arrow.
- Renaer found the side passage, handed healing potions to Davy and Aladeen, and killed one goblin through the slit.
- Davy brought Elior back with a healing potion.
- Aladeen used Vicious Mockery through the slit.
- Elior, freshly revived and filthy, killed the remaining goblin with Ice Knife.
- Davy noticed a hidden wall panel that opened into the defended rooms.

### Xanathar Hideout And Mind Flayer

- Inside the hideout, the party found straw pallets, manacles, arrow-slit rooms, and a carved eye-like symbol: a circle with ten spokes and a smaller circular indentation.
- Opening a door revealed [[People/NPCs/Grum'shar]], a muscular half-orc in dirty robes, standing over a red-blond human who appeared to be Floon.
- The room also contained [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]], a mind flayer in black robes, calmly petting an intellect devourer.
- Jonathan stepped in and tried to talk.
- Davy and Renaer hid near the entrance; Davy pointed out Floon.
- Nihiloor told Grum'shar, "You know what to do," then used psychic command to freeze Jonathan and Renaer.
- Nihiloor placed a sphere into the eye pedestal, opened a portal, stepped through it, and removed the sphere from the far side before vanishing. The portal remained briefly, then collapsed.

### Fight With Grum'shar And The Intellect Devourer

- Davy shot Grum'shar with a crossbow bolt from hiding.
- Aladeen tried to mock Nihiloor, but the mind flayer shrugged it off.
- The intellect devourer attacked Jonathan's mind and later knocked him unconscious with psychic damage.
- Grum'shar cast Burning Hands on Davy, Renaer, and Broethuul, dropping Davy.
- Broethuul shot Grum'shar with a crossbow, triggering the half-orc's refusal to fall.
- Jonathan then killed Grum'shar with a critical morningstar strike.
- Aladeen revived Davy with Healing Word, then mocked the intellect devourer.
- Renaer killed the intellect devourer with rapier and dagger.

### Floon Rescued

- Floon was alive but badly hurt and barely conscious.
- Aladeen used the remaining healing potion on Floon.
- Floon recovered enough to hug his rescuers, cling to Jonathan, and declare himself forever in their debt.
- The party looted:
  - [[Objects/Grum'shar's Spellbook]], which no one could immediately read;
  - two healing potions from a chest;
  - loose coin from the same chest.
- Davy checked for hidden exits or trap doors but found nothing obvious.
- A lower passage led to another area where Davy briefly saw [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] arguing with a deep gnome about barricading a door. Davy quietly closed the door, and the party chose not to pursue that thread.

### Return To The Yawning Portal

- The party followed the yellow signs back out and returned to the street with Renaer and Floon.
- Renaer left before entering the Yawning Portal, saying he needed to put minds at ease and asking the party to look him up anytime.
- At the [[Places/Yawning Portal]], Volo greeted Floon with visible relief.
- Volo admitted he was short on cash and could not pay the promised 100 gp per PC.
- Instead, he offered the [[Objects/Trollskull Manor Deed]] as something more valuable, promising a magistrate could witness the transfer after the party inspected the property.
- The session ended with the party locating [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] on the map and planning to inspect it next session.

## NPCs Introduced Or Changed

- [[People/NPCs/Hustus Staget]]: Watch captain for the Dock Ward; practical, respected, and more interested in keeping gang blood off the streets than sending Watch patrols into sewers.
- [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]]: Mind flayer connected to the Xanathar sewer hideout; escaped through a portal using an eye-marked pedestal and a sphere.
- [[People/NPCs/Grum'shar]]: Half-orc spellcaster who was torturing Floon; killed by Jonathan.
- [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]]: Rescued alive from the Xanathar hideout.
- [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]]: Actively helped the party, acquired healing potions, fought in the sewer, and parted on friendly terms.
- [[People/NPCs/Krentz]]: Spotted deeper in the hideout arguing with a deep gnome; still active and apparently tied to the Xanathar side.

## Clues And Information Gained

- Yellow painted circle-and-spoke signs mark the Xanathar sewer route.
- The Xanathar Guild has at least one sewer hideout under or near the Dock Ward.
- The hideout contained cells, goblin sentries, a gazer lookout, a half-orc spellcaster, an intellect devourer, and a mind flayer.
- The eye-marked pedestal and removable sphere opened a short-lived portal for Nihiloor.
- Staget's "keep the blood off the streets" guidance implies the Watch may tolerate private action against gang violence if public order is preserved.
- Volo has more assets than cash, or at least claims to.

## Combat And Consequences

- The party killed:
  - one gazer;
  - two goblin sentries;
  - Grum'shar;
  - one intellect devourer.
- Nihiloor escaped.
- Elior was dropped by goblin arrows and revived with a potion.
- Davy was dropped by Burning Hands and revived by Aladeen.
- Jonathan was dropped by psychic damage from the intellect devourer and revived with a potion.
- Floon survived, but only after healing.

## Items, Money, And Obligations

- Renaer brought three healing potions from Staget. They were spent during the rescue: on Elior, Jonathan, and Floon.
- The party found two additional healing potions in the hideout chest.
- The party recovered loose coin from the hideout chest; the transcript notes that totals were sent in chat, but the amount is not captured in the text transcript.
- The party recovered [[Objects/Grum'shar's Spellbook]] from Grum'shar's satchel.
- Volo offered [[Objects/Trollskull Manor Deed]] instead of the promised cash reward.

## End State

- Floon is rescued and alive.
- Renaer is free, grateful, and friendly to the party.
- Volo still owes the party satisfaction for the job and has offered Trollskull Manor as payment.
- The party has not yet inspected Trollskull Manor or completed the legal transfer.
- The Xanathar sewer hideout is partially explored; the lower Krentz/deep gnome area remains unresolved.
