# Loose It Then Leave Note Fragment

The Loose It Then Leave note fragment is a mostly burned clue found near the cracked-open grell crate in the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]]. Session 6 table memory clarified that Aladeen had or found this clue, though the exact recovery moment in session 5 remains transcript-muddied.

## Revealed Contents

The readable line says: "Loose it, then leave."

## Current Significance

This confirms that the grell was intentionally released and that the handlers were expected to abandon the site afterward. It supports [[People/NPCs/Vajra Safahr|Vajra's]] claim that Xanathar-linked actors use monster releases to spread panic and pull the Watch thin.

In [[Sessions/Session 06 - 2026-04-09]], the fragment briefly vanished from the party's belongings while Vajra investigated the receipt, then reappeared. Vajra instructed the party to preserve their evidence and not hand it to the Watch yet.
