# Floon's Unicorn Necklace

Floon's unicorn necklace is the conspicuous jewelry that made [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] easy to identify: a large unicorn-head medallion on a chain of blue pearls.

## Session 2 Clue Role

- Volo gave the necklace description when he hired the party.
- Davy and Broethuul noticed the broken medallion and remaining pearls in the gutter outside [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]].
- The string had snapped and many pearls were missing.
- A trail of loose blue pearls led north to [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]].
