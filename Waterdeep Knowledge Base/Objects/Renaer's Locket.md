# Renaer's Locket

Renaer's locket is a personal keepsake containing a portrait of his mother. The party found it smashed open in the upstairs office of [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]].

## Current Status

- Recovered by [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]].
- Repaired by [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] with Mending.
- Returned to Renaer, who put it back around his neck.

## Clue

The locket had a hidden compartment behind the portrait. Renaer did not know the compartment existed, and whatever was inside it was gone by the time the party found it.

## Open Questions

- What was hidden in the compartment?
- Did the Zhentarim, the Xanathar Guild, or someone else remove it?
- Was the locket itself the target, or one clue among several?
