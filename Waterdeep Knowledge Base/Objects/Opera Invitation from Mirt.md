# Opera Invitation from Mirt

The opera invitation from Mirt was delivered by [[People/NPCs/Threestrings]] as a folded paper bird that unfolded into a note on the table at [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Contents

- Renaer says the party is a good bet.
- Tickets have been bought for the opera tomorrow night at [[Places/Lightsinger Theater]] in the Sea Ward.
- If interested, the party should meet [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] at intermission in private box C.
- Formal attire is required.
