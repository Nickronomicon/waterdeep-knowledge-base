# Ulbrinter Calling Card

The Ulbrinter Calling Card was delivered to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] by [[People/NPCs/Ulbrinter Manservant]] in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]].

## Contents and Meaning

- Bore the Ulbrinter crest.
- Requested that the party visit [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] at their earliest convenience.
- The party understood the noble phrasing to mean that Remallia expected them promptly.
- The card led directly to the party's visit to [[Places/Ulbrinter Villa]].

## Related Detail

[[People/NPCs/Squiddly]] stole the manservant's Ulbrinter-crest handkerchief during the Trollskull visit.
