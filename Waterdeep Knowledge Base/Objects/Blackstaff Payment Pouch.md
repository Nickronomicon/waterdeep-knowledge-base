# Blackstaff Payment Pouch

The Blackstaff payment pouch appeared on the bar at [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] after [[People/NPCs/Vajra Safahr]] received Broethuul's report about the Dock Ward grell incident.

## Known Facts

- Cloth pouch embroidered with Blackstaff Tower.
- Contained 30 gold dragons.
- Appeared with a metallic clink shortly after Vajra's Sending exchange.
- The party treated it as tavern/roof money rather than individual shares.
