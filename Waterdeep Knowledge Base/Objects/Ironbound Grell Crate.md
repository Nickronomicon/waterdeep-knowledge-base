# Ironbound Grell Crate

The ironbound grell crate is the cracked-open container found in the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]] after the grell fight in [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]].

## Known Facts

- Ironbound and large enough to have contained the grell.
- Cracked open roughly to grell size.
- Found near the burned [[Objects/Loose It Then Leave Note Fragment]].
- Connected to the [[Objects/Dockside Transfer Receipt]], which references three ironbound crates.
- Session 6 table memory clarified that Davy found or was most associated with the crate clue.
- Vajra's follow-up report treated the crate evidence as useful and potentially worth preserving in a cleaner accounting.

## Open Questions

- Were there three creatures, one creature plus empty decoys, or a broader shipment?
- Where are the other crates?
- Who transported, paid for, and received them?
