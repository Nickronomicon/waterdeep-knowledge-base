# Thalosmere Archive Report on Remallia Haventree

This private House Thalosmere archive report gave [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] context before the party met [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]] at [[Places/Ulbrinter Villa]].

## Contents

- Remallia Haventree, also known as the Widow Ulbrinter, is a sun elf noble connected to House Ulbrinter.
- Lord Arthagast Ulbrinter's death about five years earlier explains her long public seclusion.
- Her public reputation is discretion, restraint, hospitality, and principled charity.
- The archive characterizes her as likely good, but secretive in a way that should be treated as deliberate concealment rather than eccentricity.
- The report advises Elior to speak plainly, avoid flattering grief, and watch the room.

## Table Status

The handout was revealed to Elior in session 9. Its contents are not automatically party-wide unless Elior shares them.
