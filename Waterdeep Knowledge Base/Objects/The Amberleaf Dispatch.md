# The Amberleaf Dispatch

The Amberleaf Dispatch is a private handout revealed to [[People/PCs/Broethuul]] in [[Sessions/Session 12 - 2026-07-09]]. It appeared as an autumn leaf that does not rot, delivered underwater by [[People/NPCs/Zelifarn]] after a fey or "door-smelling" visitor left it near the harbor threshold.

## Reveal Status

- Revealed privately to Broethuul in session 12.
- The wider party saw that Zelifarn gave Broethuul a strange autumn leaf.
- Broethuul did not read the message aloud during the transcript, so its contents are DM-facing and Broethuul-private unless later shared.
- `../raw/notes/handouts/s12-the-amberleaf-dispatch.md` is the session-12 revealed handout.
- `../raw/notes/handouts/the-amberleaf-dispatch-revealed.md` remains tracked as a related raw handout variant, but the session transcript did not establish that all of its wording was delivered.

## Contents Summary

The revealed handout identifies Broethuul as Starfern and frames his arrival in Waterdeep as planted rather than merely lost. It says the harbor threshold remembers him and ties the following threads together:

- The broken Crommor horn and the Stone/Eye that can swallow names and memory.
- A lost Starfern tide-compact beneath mortal Waterdeep imagery of red leaves and ship masts.
- An infernal or contract-rooted "House of Coin and Smile" whose fortune grows from ash, blood, and soil once marked by Autumn.
- The possibility that if the Eye remembers the swallowed season, Broethuul could be named Regent by right.
