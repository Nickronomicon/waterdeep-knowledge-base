# The Crommor Warning

The Crommor Warning is an unrevealed handout for [[People/PCs/Davy]]. It is a recovered copy of the message hidden inside the ceremonial bugle from [[Threads/Crommor Incident|the Crommor Incident]], originally meant for Laeral Silverhand.

## Current Status

- Target audience: [[People/PCs/Davy]].
- Revealed: no.
- Original: sealed inside the ceremonial bugle and failed to reach Laeral.
- Current form: recovered copy or draft.

## Content Summary

The warning is addressed to the person bearing Waterdeep's burden, implicitly Laeral Silverhand. It does not name House Cassalanter directly, but it describes a house whose prosperity has accelerated beyond ordinary chance and whose failures vanish too cleanly.

The writer suspects a non-mortal bargain struck around Laeral's elevation, guided by whispers promising salvation for the city while demanding hidden payments. The warning frames Waterdeep as already pledged in small, ignorable pieces and cautions Laeral to watch those who smile confidently while speaking of civic duty.

## Campaign Meaning

This source turns [[Threads/Cassalanter Warning in the Bugle]] from a candidate secret into a concrete unrevealed handout. It supports the working truth that the bugle carried a warning about infernal contracts and Cassalanter-adjacent prosperity, while preserving deniability because the text itself avoids naming a culprit.
