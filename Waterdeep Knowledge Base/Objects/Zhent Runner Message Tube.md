# Zhent Runner Message Tube

Conditional player-facing handout for session 13. Give this to the players only if they recover the message tube from the Zhentarim-adjacent runner during the street-war spillover near Trollskull Manor.

## Ready-To-Post Handout

The message is written on a narrow strip of cheap paper, rolled tight enough to fit inside a waxed message tube. The outside is smudged with blood and alley grime. The writing inside is hurried but legible.

> D.,
>
> The gnome is not dead. He changed carts twice after the yard and still asks after old Neverember channels.
>
> He has the thing, or knows where it sleeps. He keeps saying the stone remembers the road but not the eyes.
>
> Xanathar's people are on the same scent. One of their little floating watchers saw him near the north-side alleys before we lost him.
>
> If the lord's son has found a new house, watch the house. Do not bring this to the Portal. Too many ears.
>
> - Y.

## DM Notes

- This is intentionally partial. It should confirm that the Zhentarim-Xanathar street war is still tangled around a rock gnome, a stone, and Neverember channels without explaining Dalakhar or the full Fireball setup.
- `D.` can point toward Davil Starsong if the party connects it.
- `Y.` can point toward Yagra Stonefist if useful, or remain ambiguous if that would be too hot.
- "The lord's son" is Renaer Neverember.
- "A new house" can imply Trollskull Manor without proving anyone had already targeted it.
