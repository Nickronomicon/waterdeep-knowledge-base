# Stone of Golorr

DM-facing artifact page for the Stone at the center of Neverember's Enigma and the Remix's Eye heist structure.

## Current Table Status

- The party has not seen or identified the Stone directly.
- The Stone has been named in [[Objects/Scrap of Note]] and in Maxeene's rock gnome lead.
- The party has heard enough to suspect the Stone is important, but not enough to understand the Eyes, the vault, or the artifact's memory function.

## Remix Structure

- The Stone is blinded: three Eyes have been removed.
- Restoring Eyes unlocks communication, tracking, and eventually the vault-location secret.
- The missing Eyes are structurally tied to [[Factions/Cassalanters]], [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]], and Manshoon's [[Factions/Zhentarim]] unless table events change that.
- See [[Reference/Remix Golorr Artifacts]] for operating details and states.

## Current Clue Web

- [[Objects/Scrap of Note]]: "Stone", "Blinded", X, Z, Lady B, and R.
- [[Objects/Renaer's Locket]]: structurally tied to an Eye in the Remix.
- Maxeene's rock gnome lead: a gnome spoke of a stone, followed a lord's carriage, and later followed a well-dressed red-haired man.
- [[Threads/Great Game]]: Remallia framed the Stone-adjacent clues as evidence of a broader faction/noble contest.

## DM Warnings

- Do not reveal the Stone's full nature in player-facing synopsis text until the party learns it.
- Treat the Remix as structural truth, but let actual table clues determine what the players can infer.
- If custom campaign material changes the Eyes, record the retcon here and in [[Reference/Remix Revelation Lists]].
