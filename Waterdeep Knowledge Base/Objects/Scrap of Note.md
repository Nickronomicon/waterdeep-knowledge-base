# Scrap of Note

The Scrap of Note is a clue recovered from [[People/NPCs/Mr Jones]] after the Castle Ward ambush in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]].

## Revealed Contents

The note says:

> Stone secure. Blinded. X has one eye too many now, as does Z. Third eye no longer rests with Lady B. Keeping watch in the meantime. Contact R?

## Current Significance

This is the first explicit table-facing clue naming the Stone. The party immediately speculated that X might be [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]], Z might be [[Factions/Zhentarim]], and R might be [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]], but none of those readings are confirmed.

## Open Questions

- Who wrote the note?
- Who is "Lady B"?
- What are the "eyes"?
- Does "Blinded" mean the Stone, a person, or a faction status?
- Was Mr. Jones watching Mirt, the Harpers, the party, or all of them?
