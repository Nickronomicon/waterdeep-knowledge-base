# Trollskull Hiring Applications

Player-facing hiring packet for the Trollskull Manor grand opening on Ches 19, 1492 DR. This can be posted before session 13 so the party can choose which applicants to interview at the table.

## Ready-To-Post Handout

The following replies arrive in response to the Trollskull Manor hiring notice, gathered from guild boards, neighbor referrals, and people who heard there might be work in a newly reopened tavern.

### Lillian Haekin

**Applying for:** Bartender, tea service, front room service
**Availability:** Immediate, prefers regular shifts
**Experience:** Tea houses, private service, and formal drink preparation
**Notable strengths:** Calm under pressure, excellent memory for orders, graceful with fussy customers
**Request:** A clean station, decent storage for tea and spices, and warning before any truly strange magical service effects
**First impression:** Polished, steady, and quietly observant

### Mosp

**Applying for:** Bartender, specialty drinks, novelty service
**Availability:** Immediate, happy with late hours
**Experience:** Caravan work, dockside drink stalls, reptilian and draconic clientele
**Notable strengths:** Fearless with unusual ingredients, very enthusiastic about signature drinks, good with rowdy customers
**Request:** Permission to develop one house drink with "proper bite"
**First impression:** Bright blue-green kobold with total confidence and strong opinions about mammalian palates

### Patric Nivka

**Applying for:** Barback, server, porter, general help
**Availability:** Immediate, needs steady work
**Experience:** Errands, hauling, stable work, dish work, and odd jobs
**Notable strengths:** Eager, honest-seeming, physically capable, inexpensive
**Request:** An advance, a corner to sleep in, or both if the owners can spare it
**First impression:** Young Illuskan man trying very hard to look less desperate than he is

### Althaea, Elthaea, and Ulthaea

**Applying for:** Servers, room service, special events
**Availability:** As a trio only
**Experience:** Private salons, magical banquets, and performance service
**Notable strengths:** Telekinetic serving, synchronized movement, memorable presentation
**Request:** Tips are important, and they prefer not to be separated during service
**First impression:** Three floating star elf triplets who treat serving drinks like choreography

### Ryba

**Applying for:** Server, bar runner, crowd work
**Availability:** Immediate, especially evenings
**Experience:** Taprooms, market stalls, and "knowing what people are saying"
**Notable strengths:** Friendly, fast with gossip, claims not to scare easily
**Request:** A trial shift before anyone makes a final decision
**First impression:** Eager halfling with an easy smile and a lot of opinions about what people have heard about Trollskull

### Reynardo Greenleaf

**Applying for:** Cook, culinary artist, special menu consultant
**Availability:** Select shifts, event work preferred
**Experience:** Private dinners, performance dining, spell-assisted presentation
**Notable strengths:** Memorable dishes, dramatic plating, excellent for wealthy or artistic guests
**Request:** Creative control over at least one course
**First impression:** Half-elf mage who describes food as if it is theater that can be eaten

### Turgattan Thornhammer

**Applying for:** Cook, bouncer, "whatever needs doing"
**Availability:** Immediate
**Experience:** Adventuring camps, garrison kitchens, and "feeding people who complain too much"
**Notable strengths:** Confident, loud, unafraid of knives, fire, or criticism
**Request:** Respect for dwarven portions
**First impression:** Dwarf ex-adventurer with a handshake like a trap closing and no written references

## DM Notes

- Keep this packet spoiler-clean if posted before session.
- The Underboughs already cover the kitchen unless the party wants backup, spectacle, or a bad idea.
- Lif is not included because he is not applying; the party should still decide whether he is visible, hidden, or theatrical during service.
- Use interviews only for applicants the players actually care about.
