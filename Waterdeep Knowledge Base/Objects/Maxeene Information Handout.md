# Maxeene Information Handout

The Maxeene Information Handout is the revealed table record of what [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]] told the party before the [[Places/Thomril & Sons Cartage]] arson attack.

## Contents

- A sun elf and half-orc bodyguard hired Maxeene one or two days before the party interviewed her.
- Maxeene dropped them near the [[Places/Yawning Portal]].
- Their conversation concerned hiring spies to locate [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] hideouts in Waterdeep.
- The half-orc bodyguard strongly matches [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]].
- The sun elf likely matches [[People/NPCs/Davil Starsong]].
- A rock gnome had previously hired Maxeene while talking about a precious stone.
- That gnome followed a lord's carriage on one occasion and a well-dressed red-haired man on foot on another.

## Table Status

This information was passed to the party as a revealed handout in session 9 after the Maxeene mission, then reported to [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]].
