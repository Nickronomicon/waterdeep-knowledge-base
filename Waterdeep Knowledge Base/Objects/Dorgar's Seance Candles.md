# Dorgar's Seance Candles

Dorgar's Seance Candles are two off-white candles given to the party by [[People/NPCs/Dorgar]] in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]] as preparation for investigating [[Places/Trollskull Manor|Trollskull Manor's]] haunting.

## Instructions

- Light the first candle in a window of the house.
- Let it burn completely.
- Use the first candle's flame to light the second candle.
- Let the second candle burn completely.
- The process takes about a day; Dorgar recommended beginning one evening and finishing the next evening.
- Afterward, Dorgar can perform the rest of the preparation and try to determine whether the haunting is a ghost or another kind of magic.

## Session 10 Status

- Davy began the candle preparation on the morning of Ches 5, 1492 DR.
- The first candle was lit in an upstairs window at Trollskull Manor because the lower windows were boarded.
- The process was clarified as roughly 24 hours total, with each candle burning about eight hours and the flame carried from candle to candle.
- If uninterrupted, Dorgar can come to Trollskull Manor the next day.

## Session 11 Resolution

- The table clarified that the candle sequence could plausibly have started after the party returned from Dorgar rather than the following morning.
- By the evening of Ches 5, the last candle was nearly burned down, letting Dorgar attempt the seance that night.
- The preparation succeeded: Dorgar contacted [[People/NPCs/Lif]], who answered five questions and confirmed himself as the manor's ghost.

## Open Questions

- Are the burnt candle remnants useful or inert after the completed seance?
- Can this rite be repeated if the party needs deeper contact with Lif?
