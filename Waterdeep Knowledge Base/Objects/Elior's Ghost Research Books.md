# Elior's Ghost Research Books

Elior's Ghost Research Books are the three volumes [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] gathered in [[Sessions/Session 10 - 2026-06-11]] while privately pursuing Kelemvorite exorcism or ghost-removal options for [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Titles

- *Dead and Beyond*: a semi-scholarly or popular work by an author Elior recognized. House Thalosmere had a copy.
- *Domain of Death and Other Esoteric Topics*: a denser scholarly work with multiple listed authors. House Thalosmere had a copy in its deeper archives.
- *Volo's Guide to the Undead*: a newly released Volo title, purchased for 12 gp 5 sp.

## Current Status

- Elior has the books and returned to Trollskull Manor to read them.
- To the party, Elior described the research as family-history and self-knowledge work rather than openly saying he was trying to exorcise the manor's ghosts.
- Elior intends to find a way to exorcise or move on the spirits himself if possible.
