# Dockside Transfer Receipt

The Dockside Transfer Receipt is a torn cargo clue recovered from a dead handler in the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]] during [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]]. Session 6 table memory clarified that Jonathan had or found this clue, though the exact recovery moment in session 5 remains transcript-muddied.

## Revealed Contents

- Three ironbound crates were received before dawn.
- No customs inspection was requested.
- Payment was made in silver.
- The mark was one staring eye inside a ring.

## Current Significance

The receipt ties the grell release to a deliberate cargo transfer rather than a random monster appearance. The eye-in-ring mark strongly suggests [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] involvement or an intentional imitation of Xanathar methods.

In [[Sessions/Session 06 - 2026-04-09]], the receipt briefly vanished from the party's belongings while Vajra investigated its address, then reappeared. Vajra instructed the party to preserve their evidence and not hand it to the Watch yet. The exact address/name on the receipt was not captured cleanly in the transcript; prep suggests a chandlery or shipping-broker lead near Fishgut Court, but that should be confirmed from chat or later play before being treated as table truth.
