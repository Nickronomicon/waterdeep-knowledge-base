# Dockside Transfer Receipt

The Dockside Transfer Receipt is a torn cargo clue recovered from a dead handler in the [[Places/Fish Street Warehouse]] during [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]].

## Revealed Contents

- Three ironbound crates were received before dawn.
- No customs inspection was requested.
- Payment was made in silver.
- The mark was one staring eye inside a ring.

## Current Significance

The receipt ties the grell release to a deliberate cargo transfer rather than a random monster appearance. The eye-in-ring mark strongly suggests [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]] involvement or an intentional imitation of Xanathar methods.
