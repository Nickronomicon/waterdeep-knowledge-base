# Frewn's Brews

Frewn's Brews is [[People/NPCs/Emmet Frewn|Emmet Frewn's]] tavern in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]], opened after Frewn failed to buy [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Description

- Converted hastily from what appears to have been a shop.
- Smaller and less prominent than Trollskull Manor.
- Does not front the alley as well as Trollskull Manor.
- Has a poorly extended bar that still resembles a shopkeeper's counter.
- Has only two unimpressive taps.
- The roof is serviceable but visibly self-repaired.
- Had very few customers when Aladeen visited.

## Current Role

- Immediate neighborhood competitor and likely rivalry source.
- The guild appears to have concerns about the roof work.
- [[People/NPCs/Broxley Fairkettle]] says Frewn's Brews is a [[Factions/Fellowship of Innkeepers]] member but late on dues.
- On the morning of Ches 16, a fresh poster across from Trollskull advertised Frewn's Brews as opening soon with "Real drinks, living staff, no ghosts."
