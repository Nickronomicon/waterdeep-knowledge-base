# Blackstaff Tower

Blackstaff Tower is the home and seat of [[People/NPCs/Vajra Safahr]], the Blackstaff of Waterdeep. The party visited it in [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]] after Vajra contacted Jonathan by Sending.

## Current Status

- The party has visited once.
- Vajra offered the party probationary [[Factions/Gray Hands]] work there.

## Table-Facing Features

- Exterior is smooth black stone with no obvious windows and an entry that appears as a blank wall until the visitor reaches it.
- Interior is much larger than the tower appears from outside.
- Floating lanterns guide visitors.
- Stairs behave strangely: ascent may feel like descent, and the front door may appear above and far away.
- Windows show impossible weather and places rather than Castle Ward.
- Apprentices and staff pass through, including one frazzled apprentice who seemed to repeat on the stairs.
- Hallway doors changed color and handle metal after the party passed.
- Vajra's chamber is comparatively severe, ordered, and office-like.
