# Trollskull Smuggler's Passage

The Trollskull Smuggler's Passage is a hidden route connecting [[Places/Trollskull Manor|Trollskull Manor's]] cellar to the city sewer under or near [[Places/Trollskull Alley]].

## Discovery

The party discovered the passage in [[Sessions/Session 11 - 2026-06-25]] after rats erupted from a hidden wall section in Trollskull's cellar during the Underbough trial dinner.

- The first secret door opens from the cellar into a narrow earthen passage.
- The second secret door opens from that passage into the sewer.
- The sewer-side door was latched when the party reached it, and [[People/PCs/Davy]] had to force it open with thieves' tools.
- The passage showed evidence of a large number of rats moving through it.
- The party could not find another obvious entry into the passage, which suggests someone or something opened the route for the rats and closed it afterward.

## Lif's Confirmation

During Dorgar's seance, [[People/NPCs/Lif]] confirmed the passage as an old smuggler's route to the sewers. Lif said it had been mostly blocked off for years and had not been used since before his time.

## Current Status

- The cellar-side door has been temporarily blocked with junk and old barrels so that any intrusion should at least be noisy.
- The sewer-side door was opened by Davy and may need proper inspection, repair, or sealing.
- The party has not yet explored the sewer connection beyond the immediate entrance.

## Open Questions

- Who opened or used the passage to send rats into Trollskull Manor?
- Can the passage be secured without drawing guild, Watch, or underworld attention?
- Is it a liability, an escape route, an information-gathering asset, or all three?
