# Yawning Portal

The Yawning Portal is the party's starting tavern, Durnan's establishment, and the social hub that connected several PCs before the campaign began.

## Campaign Role

- [[Sessions/Session 01 - 2026-02-15]] began here with Volo's tale, the Yagra/Krentz brawl, and the troll/stirge incident.
- [[People/NPCs/Volo]] hired the party here to find [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]].
- The party's pre-campaign acquaintance web runs through [[People/NPCs/Bonnie]], [[People/NPCs/Threestrings]], and [[People/NPCs/Durnan]].
- [[Sessions/Session 03 - 2026-03-01]] ended here when the party returned Floon alive and Volo offered the [[Objects/Trollskull Manor Deed]] instead of the promised cash.
