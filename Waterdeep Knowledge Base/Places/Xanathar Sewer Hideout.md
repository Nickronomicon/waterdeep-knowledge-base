# Xanathar Sewer Hideout

The Xanathar sewer hideout is the underground location where the party rescued [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] in [[Sessions/Session 03 - 2026-03-01]]. It is reached by following yellow circle-and-spoke marks from a sewer entrance near [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]].

## Known Layout

- Yellow painted signs guide the route through the sewers.
- A gazer served as a lookout near a three-way intersection.
- The approach included a circular hub covered by arrow slits.
- Behind a hidden wall panel were defended rooms with straw pallets, rusty manacles, goblin sentries, and cell-like sleeping chambers.
- A larger chamber contained:
  - an eye-marked pedestal with a smaller central indentation;
  - [[People/NPCs/Nihiloor]];
  - [[People/NPCs/Grum'shar]];
  - an intellect devourer;
  - [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]].
- A lower passage led to another area where Davy saw [[People/NPCs/Krentz]] and a deep gnome barricading a door.

## Current State

- The party killed the gazer, two goblin sentries, Grum'shar, and the intellect devourer.
- Nihiloor escaped through a portal and took the sphere used to open it.
- Floon was rescued.
- The lower area with Krentz and the deep gnome remains unexplored by the party.

## Open Questions

- Where does the lower passage lead?
- What were Krentz and the deep gnome barricading against?
- Can the eye pedestal be activated without Nihiloor's sphere?
- Will the Xanathar Guild abandon, reinforce, or clean up this location?
