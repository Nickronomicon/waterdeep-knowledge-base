# Last Temple of Myrkul

The Last Temple of Myrkul is a hidden shrine beneath the [[Places/City of the Dead]], revealed to the party by [[People/NPCs/Dorgar]] in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]].

## Current Status

- Secret and dangerous to expose.
- Accessed by a hidden stair behind or within a mausoleum in the City of the Dead.
- Marked at the entry with a triangle-and-skull symbol recognized by [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] as Myrkul's.
- Simple rather than murderously ornate: bare altar, skull relief, black altar cloth, and a side passage.
- Described by Dorgar as the last temple of Myrkul in Waterdeep, after other temples were destroyed by followers of the Lightbringer.

## Session 9 Events

- Dorgar brought the party here only after asking them not to speak of what they saw outside the grounds.
- The party learned that Dorgar has a hidden religious role beyond his public groundskeeper identity.
- Dorgar agreed to help with Trollskull Manor's spirits and gave the party two ritual candles for preparation.
