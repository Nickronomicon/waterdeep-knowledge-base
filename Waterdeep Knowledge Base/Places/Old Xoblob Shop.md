# Old Xoblob Shop

Old Xoblob Shop is a purple curiosity shop across from the Skewered Dragon.

## Description

- Deep purple facade.
- Plush beholder in the window.
- Interior filled with lavender smoke and dyed-purple knickknacks.
- Run by [[People/NPCs/Xoblob]], a deep gnome equally committed to purple.

## Session 2 Events

- Xoblob saw Floon and another noble jumped outside the shop.
- The attackers wore dark cloaks and black leather armor.
- One attacker had a black hand tattoo that might have been Zhentarim.
- Xoblob pointed the party north.
- The broken remains of [[Objects/Floon's Unicorn Necklace]] were found in the gutter just outside.

## Session 9 Events

- The party returned while searching for someone who could speak with ghosts.
- [[People/NPCs/Xoblob]] suggested that warlocks or necromancers might be better avenues, while warning that openly seeking necromancers is a bad idea in Waterdeep.
- [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] noticed and tried a bright purple lute in the window. It was mundane but decent, badly out of tune, and offered at 60 gp, later bargained down to 45 gp.
- Aladeen did not buy the lute in session 9.
