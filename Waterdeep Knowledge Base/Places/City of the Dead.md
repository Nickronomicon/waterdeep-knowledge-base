# City of the Dead

The City of the Dead is Waterdeep's great cemetery. It entered active campaign play in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]] when [[People/PCs/Davy]] led the party there to find [[People/NPCs/Dorgar]].

## Session 9 Events

- The party arrived at or after sunset after asking [[People/NPCs/Xoblob]] about ghost communication.
- Dorgar was working on the south side of the cemetery, sweeping headstones in black robes.
- Davy introduced Dorgar as a dear friend.
- Dorgar led the party to a hidden stair behind or within a mausoleum after asking for secrecy.
- The stair led down to the [[Places/Last Temple of Myrkul]], a hidden shrine beneath the cemetery.

## Open Questions

- How securely hidden is the Myrkulite shrine?
- Which cemetery workers, priests, or other dead-adjacent figures know Dorgar's true role?
