# Drowned Lantern

The Drowned Lantern is a Dock Ward tavern or dockside bar with a private dock area. It served as the contact point for the session 12 [[Factions/Gray Hands]] assignment to investigate the bronze dragon in [[Places/Deepwater Harbor]].

## Current Status

- [[People/NPCs/Laraelra Harsard|Elra Harsard]] met the party here with water-breathing potions.
- The south side opens toward a pier or private dock.
- A Xanathar-linked salvage crew attacked the party here after realizing they had dragon-hunting or dragon-investigation potions.
- Elra arranged or implied that the Blackstaff's people would handle the bodies afterward.
