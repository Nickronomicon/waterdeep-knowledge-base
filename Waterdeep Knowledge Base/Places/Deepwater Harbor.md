# Deepwater Harbor

Deepwater Harbor is Waterdeep's harbor and the site of the session 12 bronze-dragon investigation for the [[Factions/Gray Hands]].

## Current Status

- [[People/NPCs/Zelifarn]], a young bronze dragon, is currently living around an old wreck in the harbor.
- Zelifarn does not appear hostile to the city, but the Blackstaff's people are aware of him and watching.
- The party reached the wreck using water-breathing potions supplied by [[People/NPCs/Laraelra Harsard]].
- Zelifarn reported seeing a strange fey or door-smelling visitor come through a wrong-water threshold and leave [[Objects/The Amberleaf Dispatch]] for [[People/PCs/Broethuul]].
