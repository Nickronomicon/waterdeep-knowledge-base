# Lightsinger Theater

Lightsinger Theater is the Sea Ward theater named in the opera invitation delivered to the party in session 4.

## Current Status

- The party has tickets for the following night after session 4, likely Ches 3, 1492 DR.
- They are supposed to meet [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] at intermission in private box C.
- Formal attire is required for admittance.
- The party attended the opera in [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]] and met Mirt at intermission.
- The party left during intermission to chase a watcher and did not return before the transcript ended.

## Session 6 Lead-In

- Session 6 did not reach the theater despite theater-heavy prep.
- The party spent the afternoon handling Trollskull, neighbors, evidence, and formalwear.
- Start session 7 from the party's arrival at or approach to the Lightsinger Theater, with Elior's rejoin/outfit still to confirm.

## Session 7

- Presented as a Castle Ward theater of white stone, midnight blue and gold banners, gilt balconies, velvet drapery, crystal lamps, and elite social traffic.
- The opera was *The Fall of Tiamat*, sung in Giant.
- The party's seats were good but below noble-box level.
- Box C was Mirt's private box and the site of the Harper recruitment.
- A watcher observed Box C and the party during the first act and fled through backstage at intermission, leading to the Castle Ward ambush.
