# Cassalanter Villa

Cassalanter Villa is the Sea Ward home of [[Factions/Cassalanters|House Cassalanter]]. [[People/NPCs/Victoro Cassalanter]] invited the party there during [[Sessions/Session 07 - 2026-04-16]] to discuss a possible loan for [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].

## Current Status

- Visited in [[Sessions/Session 10 - 2026-06-11]] by Aladeen, Broethuul, and Jonathan.
- Active source of tavern financing through the pending [[Objects/Cassalanter Loan]].
- Earlier notes placed the villa on Diamond Street; session 10 table mapping placed it on Delzorin Street in the Sea Ward, on the same street as Ulbrinter Villa but in a different ward. Keep this discrepancy open until confirmed.

## Description

- Stark white walls, gleaming crimson roof and turrets, picturesque green gardens, hedges, and water features.
- Surrounded by a tall white brick wall with a single wrought-iron gate.
- Gate guards control entry.
- The Cassalanter crest on the gate was described as a green Y overlapping a stylized goose being fed.
- The foyer has marble floors, winged staircases, expensive tapestries, art, statues, and an overt display of wealth.

## Session 10 Events

- Jonathan grabbed the gate and demanded entry, which prompted the guards to call the visitors peasants and block him.
- Aladeen salvaged the introduction by naming Jonathan, Broethuul, Elior, and the opera invitation.
- A servant admitted the party after checking inside.
- Victoro received the group in a salon and negotiated the loan terms.
- The meeting ended with an expensive brandy toast to business.
