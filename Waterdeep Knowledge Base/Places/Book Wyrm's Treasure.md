# Book Wyrm's Treasure

Book Wyrm's Treasure is a warm, crowded bookshop in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]], run by [[People/NPCs/Rishaal the Page-Turner]].

## Table-Facing Description

- Sign shows a gold dragon curled around a treasure hoard made of books and scrolls.
- Interior has beautiful hardwood furniture and shelves, with the earthy scent of old books.
- The shop spills upward to a second floor and seems to hold more shelves than the exterior suggests.

## Current Role

- Source of books, curious texts, and scrolls.
- Loaned Aladeen [[Objects/Borrowed Monster Books]] in session 6.
- Aladeen remembered in session 10 that those borrowed books still need to be returned.
