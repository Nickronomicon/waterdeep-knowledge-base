# Ulbrinter Villa

Ulbrinter Villa is the home of [[People/NPCs/Remallia Haventree]], widow of Lord Arthagast Ulbrinter. It became an active Harper contact site in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]].

## Description

- Located in the North Ward, with the Thalosmere archive placing House Ulbrinter on Delzorin Street.
- Close enough to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]] for the party to visit the same afternoon they received Remallia's calling card.
- Old stone, ivy, clean windows, drawn curtains, and precise servants gave the impression of a sleeping household rather than an abandoned one.
- The party was received in a pale morning room overlooking a bare garden.
- Subtle Harper symbols were worked into the room's carved trim, deniable as decoration to anyone not looking carefully.

## Session 9 Events

- Remallia received the party here after [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] told her they had spoken with [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]].
- She requested exact wording and direct observations rather than conclusions.
- The party reported Maxeene's information about [[People/NPCs/Davil Starsong]], [[People/NPCs/Yagra Stonefist]], and the rock gnome Stone lead.
- The party showed or described [[Objects/Scrap of Note]], which Remallia had not yet been told about.
- Remallia gave the party practical Harper support while keeping direct ties deniable.
