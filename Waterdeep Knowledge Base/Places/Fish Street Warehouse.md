# Fish Street Warehouse

The Fish Street warehouse is the Dock Ward site where the party found and killed a grell in [[Sessions/Session 05 - 2026-04-01]]. Its ownership chain has been redacted from public records.

## Current Status

- Grell killed inside.
- Two apparent handlers/thugs dead and partially consumed.
- [[People/NPCs/Armet]] rescued and healed.
- The party left before the Watch arrived.
- Ownership record redacted from the public chain available through the [[Factions/Guild of Scriveners, Scribes, and Clerks]].

## Known Features

- Near Fish Street/Fishgut Court in the Dock Ward.
- Valuable warehouse real estate, but inactive while surrounding warehouses were working.
- Side door had an industrial lock that Davy picked.
- Nearby storm-drain style sewer grate was bent upward, with scrape marks suggesting a release route or forced movement through the drain.
- Contained an [[Objects/Ironbound Grell Crate|ironbound crate]] cracked open to roughly grell size.
- Contained a burned [[Objects/Loose It Then Leave Note Fragment]].
- A torn [[Objects/Dockside Transfer Receipt]] was recovered from one ruined body.
