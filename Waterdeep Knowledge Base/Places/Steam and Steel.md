# Steam and Steel

Steam and Steel is a busy forge in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]], operated by [[People/NPCs/Embric]] and [[People/NPCs/Avi]].

## Current Role

- Neighborhood forge for weapons, armor, shields, tools, and smithing work.
- First visited by the party in [[Sessions/Session 10 - 2026-06-11]].
- Carries standard metal weapons, armor, and shields at guild-listed prices.
- Open to trade or barter when value is equivalent and the guild cut is respected.

## Description

- Smoke and steam billow from the windows.
- The forge is indoors but impressively sound-managed; hammering is much quieter from outside than expected.
- Embric was working on a huge sword when the party arrived.
- Avi was tending the forge around a large shield.

## Session 10 Events

- [[People/PCs/Aladeen]] brought [[People/PCs/Jonathan Usman Sonny Theodore Icarus Charles Eagleshield|Jonathan]], the urchins, and others while checking whether [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] had been seen.
- Embric and Avi had not seen Elior that morning.
- Aladeen sold a spear for 4 sp, leaving the credit on a Trollskull account.
- Aladeen considered studded leather and training shields for the urchins but did not buy them here.
- Embric and Avi expressed interest in visiting Trollskull Manor for drinks when it opens.
