# Tiger's Eye

Tiger's Eye is a discreet private investigation office in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]], run by [[People/NPCs/Vincent Trench]].

## Table-Facing Description

- Unremarkable building with an orange-and-black cat's-eye sign.
- Door was locked when Aladeen visited, with a bell beside it.
- Interior is a mixed living and work space: well-decorated, dimly lit, with a receiving room and office.

## Current Use

- Potential paid investigation resource for the gazer/Xanathar surveillance around Trollskull Manor.
- Vincent has not yet been retained.
