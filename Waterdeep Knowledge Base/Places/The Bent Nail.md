# The Bent Nail

The Bent Nail is a neat woodshop in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]] run by [[People/NPCs/Tally Fellbranch]], a skilled carpenter and woodcarver.

## Current Role

- Source for local carpentry, furniture, carved pieces, and woodwork.
- Neighboring business to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Potential practical ally for tavern restoration, though no work has been commissioned yet.
