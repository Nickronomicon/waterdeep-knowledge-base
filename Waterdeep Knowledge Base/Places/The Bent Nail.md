# The Bent Nail

The Bent Nail is a neat woodshop in [[Places/Trollskull Alley]] run by [[People/NPCs/Tally Fellbranch]], a skilled carpenter and woodcarver.

## Current Role

- Source for local carpentry, furniture, carved pieces, and woodwork.
- Neighboring business to [[Places/Trollskull Manor]].
- Practical ally for tavern restoration and child-training bucklers.

## Session 10 Events

- The party visited looking for inexpensive wooden training bucklers for the urchins.
- [[People/NPCs/Tally Fellbranch]] agreed to make three wooden bucklers for 10 gp total, ready the next day.
- After negotiating the Cassalanter loan, the party stopped back by and told Tally to come by because they would soon have business for him.
