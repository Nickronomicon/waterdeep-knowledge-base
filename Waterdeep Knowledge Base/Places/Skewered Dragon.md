# Skewered Dragon

The Skewered Dragon is a seedy Dock Ward tavern where [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] was last seen before his abduction.

## Description

- A bar with no reputation, which the DM framed as worse than a bad reputation.
- Located near [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]], facing an alley between Net Street and Fillet Lane.
- Looks like a ruin, with smashed front windows and a ship's anchor lodged in the broken roof.
- Serves rough dockworkers, sailors, and regulars who are hard to impress.
- The bartender is mute, scarred, missing fingers, and likely has a peg leg.

## Session 2 Events

- The party questioned regular dockworkers here.
- Witnesses said Floon was drunk, loud, and out of place.
- A second noble arrived and left with Floon.
- Four or five rough men followed them after Midnight Bell; one had a black snake tattoo.
- Broethuul nearly started a bar fight, but the situation turned into a coin scramble.
