# Dock Ward

The Dock Ward is the rougher waterfront part of Waterdeep where session 2's Floon investigation unfolded.

## Table-Facing Description

- Rougher than the Sea Ward or Castle Ward.
- Home to sailors, dockworkers, and longshoremen.
- Shady business is common and may go uninvestigated.
- Recent underworld violence has made street bodies less shocking than they would have been a month earlier.
