# The Plinth

The Plinth is an all-faiths temple in Waterdeep's Trades Ward, south of the [[Places/City of the Dead]]. The party visited it in [[Sessions/Session 09 - 2026-05-07]] while looking for help communicating with Trollskull Manor's spirits.

## Description

- A damaged or diminished version of a once-taller religious structure, with its spires collapsed after the Spellplague.
- Now resembles a ruined coliseum or stub of a much larger spire.
- Hosts altars and representatives for many faiths rather than one dominant temple.
- Includes a Kelemvorite altar served by [[People/NPCs/Brother Senric]].

## Session 9 Events

- [[People/PCs/Elior Thalosmere]] identified possible religious approaches to ghost questions: Mystra or Oghma for magic and knowledge, Kelemvor for the dead, and Lathander as a faith likely to treat undead very harshly.
- The party chose to approach Kelemvor's altar.
- Brother Senric could not speak with spirits and instead urged the party to consider helping any lingering souls move onward.
- The party left without securing ghost communication and continued to [[Places/Old Xoblob Shop]].
