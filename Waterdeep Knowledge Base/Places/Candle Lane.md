# Candle Lane

Candle Lane is a Dock Ward warehouse lane north of Old Xoblob Shop.

## Session 2 Details

- Davy overheard that Candle Lane warehouses had become more dangerous and that about half had shut down.
- Floon's scattered blue pearls led the party here.
- The lane was dark, with smashed street lamps and one remaining lamp flickering in the distance.
- [[Places/Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse]] stood at the end of the trail.
