# Thomril & Sons Cartage

Thomril & Sons Cartage is a cartage stable outside Waterdeep's North Gate/near Undercliff. The party visited it in [[Sessions/Session 08 - 2026-04-30]] to interview [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]] for [[People/NPCs/Mirt]] and the [[Factions/Harpers]].

## Current Status

- The party reached Thomril & Sons on the morning after the Castle Ward ambush.
- The location included a stable, attached shop, drays/sledges, lumber posts, an anvil/horse-shoeing setup, and a dung heap.
- [[People/NPCs/Maxeene]] was confirmed as a talking draft mare and Harper contact.
- During the interview, attackers came to burn the place down and kill everyone there.
- The stablemaster/owner was killed during the fight, likely [[People/NPCs/Whelan Thomril]] from prep, though the transcript did not capture his name at the table.
- By the end of session 8, the stable was burning badly and its final condition had not been resolved.

## Session 8 Attack

- Attackers discussed whether the spy worked there before setting the building on fire.
- The fire-obsessed gnome caster used fire magic heavily and accelerated the burn.
- One attacker bore a flying-snake tattoo and used poisoned weapons, pointing toward [[Factions/Zhentarim]] involvement.
- [[People/NPCs/Thugby]] was recognized among the attackers and fled after recognizing the party.
- Maxeene survived and killed one attacker herself.

## Open Questions

- What legal or Watch consequences follow the arson and death of the stablemaster?
- Who ordered the attack?
- Does Thomril & Sons survive as a business after the fire?
