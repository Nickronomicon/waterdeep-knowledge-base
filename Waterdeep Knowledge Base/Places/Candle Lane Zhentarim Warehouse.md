# Candle Lane Zhentarim Warehouse

The Candle Lane Zhentarim warehouse is the safe house where the party found [[People/NPCs/Renaer Neverember]] and learned that [[People/NPCs/Floon Blagmaar]] had likely been taken by [[Factions/Xanathar Guild]].

## Description

- Two-story warehouse on Candle Lane.
- Marked with a black winged snake near the front doors.
- Fenced yard with a gate standing ajar.
- A dead flying snake with an arrow through it lay in the yard.
- Interior contained scattered bodies, broken crates, old pickled herring remnants, and signs of safe-house use.
- Upstairs contained an office, alarm bell wiring, a chair with rope bonds, [[Objects/Renaer's Locket]], and [[Objects/Magical Paper from Candle Lane Warehouse]].

## Session 2 Events

- Party followed the trail from [[Objects/Floon's Unicorn Necklace]] here.
- Jonathan kicked open the doors and triggered a kenku ambush.
- Party killed four kenku and let one panicked kenku flee.
- Renaer was found hidden under a tarp in the storage room below the stairs.
- Session ended with hobnailed boots approaching the warehouse.
