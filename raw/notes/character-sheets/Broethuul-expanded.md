# Broethuul

A lot of Broethuul's text gets cut off on the exported character sheet, so I'm including it here.

## Background

**Faewild Exile**

*Feature: Position of Privilege*

Thanks to your noble birth, people are inclined to think the best of you. You are welcome in high society, and people assume you have the right to be wherever you are. The common folk make every effort to accommodate you and avoid your displeasure, and other people of high birth treat you as a member of the same social sphere. You can secure an audience with a local noble if you need to.

## Personality Traits

* Jovial
* Flirtatious
* Extremely Lazy
* Aristocratic

Broethuul is a classic aristocratic teenager become 21 with little mentorship and a lazy streak a mile wide. Unlike most bugbears, being of former Fae nobility, Broethuul is not a large beast like most bugbears. Brought up in court in the ways of etiquette, fencing, dancing and his clan's form of martial arts, Broethuul is your classic aristocratic dandy. Or he tries to be.

## Ideals
Broethuul was brought up knowing that he was part of an exiled court in hiding (the Ao Shay Court or the Court of the Outsiders). He was taught that clan Starfern was the rightful rulers of the Autumn court and, though he wasn't the oldest, he should likely be a duke in the Faewild. At the age of 16 the Starfern court was discovered and mostly wiped out. Broethuul was spirited to safety by loyal elite guard and sent through a portal into Faerun. He has no knowledge of what happened to the clan after that but his primary goal is to amass power amongst the world of the humanoids and return to his Courts hidden palace and avenge the massacre that happened there, taking his rightful place as nobility of the Autumn Court. But that sounds like a lot of work and he isn't so sure how to do that, even after living in Waterdeep for 5 years. In the mean times, japes, wine and capers keep him busy, unless something catches his interest, then he will obsess over it as only a denizen of the faewild can. 

## Bonds
Threestrings of the Yawning Portal is one of Broethuul's favorite humanoids. She reminds him of the constant revelry of a fae court, always playing music and encouraging carousing. If only she knew some good old fae tunes, humanoid music is somewhat boring.

## Flaws
Extremely lazy. If the stakes aren't high enough, it is quite likely that Broethuul will 'forget' to do something if the effort seems too high to him. His friends and acquaintances notice that he often tries to pawn of work by asking them to join in and 'share the load' while doing almost nothing himself. While he is generally more charming than the average humanoid (obviously) his fae heritage makes him a bit strange to most and many are put off by his constant attempts to pawn off work. 

An aristocrat of an aristocracy no one knows about. Very few people will know much of what he speaks or recognize the names he drops, yet his behavior is easily recognizable as aristocratic. Unfortunately he escaped with nothing but the clothes he was wearing and over 5 years, those clothes have become quite shabby. Some people believe him a fake, others don't know what to believe but let it lie since he is fun to carouse with, but many see the way he carries himself and hear how he talks and immediately defer to him since that is all the common folk have ever known how to do.

## Organizations
Starfern Fae clan, leaders of the Ao Shay Court, rightful rulers of the Autumn Court.

## Enemies
An unknown enemy organization. Probably a fae court. Agents of this court are on the lookout for Broethuul but it is very rare for them to spend lots of time in the humanoid realm amongst the humanoids. However, in the wilds of faerun were he to stray near a fae portal, or were one of the guilds of Waterdeep to discover his value to a certain fae clan...

## Backstory
Broethuul was raised in a hidden keep deep the Haunted Aielwood of the Faewild. Amongst the giant trees of the Aielwood even the light from a summers day struggles to reach the forest floor. The ruins of a great city from a civilization unknown lay hidden amongst the giant trees and fungi of the Aielwood. It was here that the Starfern clan built it's new home hidden away from the hight courts of the Faewild. 

Educated as Fae nobility, with a broad but not deep understanding of the histories and the arcane, alongside the constant carousing and debauchery that is common in the fae courts, Broethuul excelled in all things a young man actually cared about, fencing, sneaking and spying on others of the court, dancing and so on. 

In his studies Broethuul heard from all of his tutors that the Starfern clan was the rightful rulers of the Autumn court but another clan had tricked the Seelie and Unseelie into elevating them over the Starfern and exiling the Starfern. While this was drilled into a young Broethuul it seemed so far away that it was just another history to learn just enough of to make his tutors let him go to the hall and open more wine with his friends. 

At the age of 16, those histories became all too real as the Starfern keep was stormed. Broethuul was whisked away and never saw who the attackers were. He was taken to a portal to the humanoid realm and sent through, told that he should hide until the clan finds him and regroups. Broethuul finds himself swimming in saltwater on the other side of the portal. He manages to get to the surface and get picked up by a local ketch. 

That was fiver years ago.  Since then Broethuul has learned that he appeared in an area of Waterdeep's harbor known as Umberlee's Cache, a network of underwater tunnels near the ruins of a sunken tower with a strong underwater vortex. Try as he might, Broethuul has not been able to explore the tunnels to try and find the portal.

In the mean time he has eeked out a living amongst the humanoids through japery, delightful chaos and sometimes performance with his flute. To his dismay the watch labels his activities as thieving, crime and noise ordinance violations. Still he has avoided the dungeons, only spending a night or two behind bars chatting up the poor watch on duty. When not working to bring merry japes to the masses he can be found most frequently at the Yawning Portal trying to recreate the carousing of a fae court.
