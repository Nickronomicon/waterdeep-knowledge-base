# What Broethuul remembers of the Crommor Incident
Five years ago, when you emerged from the vortex beneath Waterdeep’s harbor, something shifted. The seam between Autumn and the mortal world thinned. Since then, subtle currents of fate seem to bend around that harbor.

The day a noble girl fell at Laeral’s ascension, you felt it again. That same distortion. Not fey. Not natural.

Something binding. Something contractual.

Obviously, you ignored it.

Now, rumors of infernal dealings, fallen houses, and hidden treasure are stirring the city. And the currents feel familiar.
