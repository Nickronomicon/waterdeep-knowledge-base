# Businesses of Trollskull Alley
## The Bent Nail
> A neat woodshop run by Talisolvanar “Tally” Fellbranch, a skilled carpenter and woodcarver. The shop is known for furniture, carved pieces, and other fine wooden goods.
## Steam and Steel
> A busy forge operated by Embric and Avi, a married couple with complementary talents in metalwork. Locals come here for weapons, armor, tools, and smithing work.
## Corellon’s Crown
> A stately herbalist’s shop run by Fala Lefaliir. It is known for remedies, teas, useful plants, and its bright upper greenhouse.
## Tiger’s Eye
> A discreet private investigation office run by Vincent Trench. Those with delicate questions or missing information sometimes come here seeking answers.
## Book Wyrm’s Treasure
> A warm, crowded bookshop run by Rishaal the Page-Turner. Its shelves are packed with old volumes, curious texts, and more than a little dust.
## Frewn’s Brews
> ~~A hastily thrown-together~~ newly opened tavern run by Emmet Frewn, set in what appears to have once been a merchant’s shop. It has the look of a place trying very hard to become part of the alley’s business life.
