You are not in the Yawning Portal by accident.

House Thalosmere does not gamble, it observes.

The Yawning Portal is the most efficient place in Waterdeep to monitor irregularities:
* Unusual magic.
* Displaced prophecy.
* Souls behaving inconsistently.
* Political incidents that resolve too neatly.

Your family calls this “field research,” but you understand it as pattern recognition. You are here to listen. You are also here because something has been wrong for some time, and lately, your scar has been warm more often.

## The Scar
The seam along your spine is not holy ornament.

It reacts when:
* Divine magic produces contradictory outcomes.
* Fate bends abruptly.
* A truth is displaced rather than erased.
* A soul occupies the wrong narrative space.

It does not flare dramatically in this company, it warms.
Measured. Controlled. Like an annotation being added to a ledger.
You have learned the gradations:

|||
|-|-|
|**Faint warmth**|tolerated anomaly.|
|**Steady heat**|unresolved misalignment.|
|**Sudden spike**|correction event underway.|

## Why This Company Matters
You did not intend to form a fixed circle. It became statistically efficient to remain near them.

### Jonathan Eagleshield

Another Aasimar. His divine presence is structured, affirmed, properly categorized. When he channels divine power, your scar warms lightly, not as a warning, but in contrast. He feels filed. You do not.

### Aladeen
A satirist who reshapes narrative flow.

When he references the Crommor scandal, your scar increases in temperature slightly, not because of humor, but because he redirects consequence into performance.
Narrative correction, even voluntary, registers.

### Davy
A quiet youth with sharp instincts.

Proximity produces sustained warmth.Not hostile, not spiking, just persistent. As if something about him is not placed where it should be, however, you do not know what.

### Threestrings
A bard with unusual timing and selective intervention.

When he steers conversations or subtly redirects events, the warmth registers. Controlled interference or narrative management.

Your scar notices.

### Bonnie
Bonnie, the lovely barmaid

Her existence represents an ongoing identity divergence. The scar does not spike, it acknowledges.

A stable paradox.

### Broethuul

A stranded Bugbear of Fae nobility.

His presence is different from the others. Not divine, not infernal. Fey.

Your scar reacts inconsistently around him. At times, nothing, then at times, a thin, cool tension, as if two systems are overlapping but not syncing.

The Feywild operates on story-logic rather than law-logic. Your function reconciles contradiction, but his nature embodies it. When Broethuul speaks of titles, courts, or obligations in the language of the Fey, the warmth shifts sideways. Not hotter, but misaligned. He does not feel like an error.

He feels like a foreign filing system.

## What You Know (And Do Not Know)
You do not know which of them is the primary anomaly.

You do not know whether the warmth signals danger, correction, or convergence.

You only know:

When seated at that table in the Yawning Portal, the ledger feels active. The warmth has increased in frequency, and whatever function you represent is not dormant anymore.

Someone here is misfiled, possibly several someones. You remain to observe, and, if necessary…

To reconcile.
